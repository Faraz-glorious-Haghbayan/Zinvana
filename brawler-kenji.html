Kenji

The Honorable Samurai

Assassin Type
Legendary Rarity
4,400 Health

Overview

Kenji is an honorable samurai assassin who brings ancient warrior traditions to the modern battlefield through his masterful katana techniques and code of honor. This disciplined fighter excels at precise melee combat, swift strikes, and maintaining battlefield honor while eliminating enemies with surgical precision. Kenji's gameplay revolves around timing, positioning, and executing perfect sword techniques with deadly efficiency. His samurai training allows him to read enemy movements and respond with lightning-fast counterattacks. Kenji combines traditional warrior honor with lethal assassin skills to become a formidable melee fighter who respects his enemies while defeating them decisively.

Abilities

Attack: Katana Slash

Main Attack

Kenji performs a swift katana slash that deals high damage and gains increased damage for each consecutive hit on the same target.

Damage: 840
Range: 4.5
Reload: 1.2s
Combo bonus: +20%

Super: Honor Strike

Super Ability

Kenji dashes forward with incredible speed, striking all enemies in his path and gaining damage immunity during the dash.

Dash damage: 2,000
Dash distance: 7 tiles
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Blade Dance

Kenji's attack speed increases by 15% for each enemy eliminated, stacking up to 5 times and lasting for 8 seconds.

Warrior's Spirit

Kenji gains 25% damage reduction and immunity to crowd control effects when his health drops below 40%.

Gadgets

Whirlwind

Kenji spins his katana, creating a whirlwind that damages all nearby enemies and reflects projectiles for 3 seconds.

Smoke Bomb

Kenji throws a smoke bomb that creates a concealing cloud, making him invisible and increasing his movement speed by 30%.

Strategy & Tips

⚔️

Combo Mastery

Focus on landing consecutive hits on the same target to maximize your damage output through combo bonuses.

🎯

Precision Timing

Use Honor Strike to engage enemies or escape danger while gaining immunity to damage during the dash.

⚡

Elimination Chain

With Blade Dance, chain eliminations to gain maximum attack speed and dominate teamfights.

🛡️

Last Stand

Use Warrior's Spirit to your advantage by fighting more aggressively when at low health.

🌪️

Defensive Spin

Use Whirlwind to defend against multiple enemies and reflect dangerous projectiles back at attackers.

💨

Stealth Approach

Use Smoke Bomb to approach enemies undetected or escape when overwhelmed by multiple opponents.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Perfect for close-quarters combat and using Honor Strike to quickly eliminate isolated enemies.

Gem Grab

Excellent for protecting the gem carrier and eliminating enemies who get too close to your team.

Brawl Ball

Great for controlling the ball area and using dash abilities to quickly reach the goal.
