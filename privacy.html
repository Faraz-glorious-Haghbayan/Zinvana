Overview

Welcome to Zinvana! We are committed to protecting your privacy and being completely transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our free personality quiz platform.

Our Privacy Promise

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. That's why we've designed Zinvana to be privacy-first, requiring no sign-ups, collecting minimal data, and never selling your information to third parties.

By using Zinvana, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We will only use your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services. Here's exactly what we collect:

Information You Provide Directly

  • Quiz Responses: Your answers to personality quiz questions (stored temporarily and anonymously)
  • Feedback: Any feedback or suggestions you voluntarily provide
  • Contact Information: Only if you choose to contact us directly

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Basic Analytics: Page views, time spent on site, and general usage patterns
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, and screen resolution for optimization
  • Performance Data: Loading times and error reports to improve site performance
What We DON'T Collect
  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Social media profiles or connections
  • Financial or payment information
  • Biometric data or photos

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Primary Uses

  • Provide Quiz Results: Generate and display your personality quiz results
  • Improve User Experience: Optimize site performance and user interface
  • Content Development: Create new quizzes and improve existing ones
  • Technical Maintenance: Monitor site performance and fix technical issues

Analytics and Insights

  • Anonymous Statistics: Understand which quizzes are most popular
  • Performance Monitoring: Ensure fast loading times and smooth user experience
  • Error Detection: Identify and fix bugs or technical problems
What We Never Do
  • Sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Create detailed user profiles for tracking
  • Share individual quiz results with anyone

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may share information in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

  • Hosting Services: Secure hosting providers who store our website data
  • Analytics Tools: Privacy-focused analytics to understand site usage
  • Content Delivery: Services that help deliver our content faster globally

Legal Requirements

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or legal process
  • Safety Protection: To protect the rights, property, or safety of Zinvana or others
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues
Third-Party Commitments

Any third-party service providers we work with are required to maintain the same level of data protection and privacy standards that we uphold. They are contractually bound to use data only for the specific services they provide to us.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our site is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • Regular Updates: We keep our systems updated with the latest security patches
  • Access Controls: Limited access to data on a need-to-know basis

Data Minimization

  • Temporary Storage: Quiz data is stored only as long as necessary
  • Anonymous Processing: Personal identifiers are removed from analytics data
  • Regular Cleanup: Automatic deletion of unnecessary data
Security Limitations

While we implement strong security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information, but we are committed to protecting it with industry-standard practices.

Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control

  • Right to Know: Request information about what data we have collected
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information

Data Portability

  • Export Data: Request your data in a portable format
  • Transfer Rights: Move your data to another service (if applicable)

Opt-Out Rights

  • Analytics Opt-Out: Disable analytics tracking in your browser
  • Cookie Control: Manage cookie preferences
  • Communication Preferences: Control how we contact you
Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our privacy email. We will respond to your request within 30 days and verify your identity before processing any requests.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and quiz progress
  • Security Cookies: Protect against fraud and maintain site security
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor site performance and loading times

Analytics Cookies

  • Usage Analytics: Understand how visitors use our site (anonymized)
  • Error Tracking: Identify and fix technical problems
  • Performance Monitoring: Ensure optimal site performance

Cookie Management

  • Browser Controls: Disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Selective Blocking: Block specific types of cookies
  • Opt-Out Tools: Use privacy-focused browser extensions
Cookie Notice

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels. Our cookies are designed to improve your experience while maintaining your privacy. You can still use most of our site features even with cookies disabled.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Here's how we handle updates:

Notification Process

  • Update Notice: Clear notification on our website when changes are made
  • Email Notification: If you've provided contact information, we'll email you about significant changes
  • Effective Date: All changes include a clear effective date

Your Options

  • Review Changes: Take time to review any updates before they take effect
  • Continue Using: Continued use of our site means acceptance of the updated policy
  • Discontinue Use: Stop using our services if you disagree with changes
Version History

We maintain a history of significant changes to this policy. You can request information about previous versions and what has changed by contacting us.

Questions About Privacy?

We're here to help! If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to reach out.
