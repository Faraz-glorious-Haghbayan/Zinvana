Buzz

The Lifeguard

Assassin Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,760 Health

Overview

Buzz is a dedicated lifeguard who brings aquatic rescue skills to the battlefield as a formidable assassin. His unique hook mechanics allow him to pull enemies close for devastating melee attacks, making him excellent at isolating and eliminating priority targets. Buzz's ability to traverse water and use his lifeguard training for combat makes him a versatile close-range fighter. His protective instincts and rescue expertise translate into aggressive battlefield control.

Abilities

Attack: Torpedo Toss

Main Attack

Buzz throws his torpedo in an arc, dealing damage to enemies in the impact area. The torpedo has a medium range and splash damage.

Damage: 1,400
Range: 6
Splash radius: 1.33
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Rescue Buoy

Super Ability

Buzz throws his rescue buoy, which hooks the first enemy hit and pulls them close while stunning them briefly.

Hook damage: 800
Stun duration: 1s
Range: 9
Charges: Passive

Star Powers

Tougher Torpedo

Buzz's torpedo pierces through enemies and deals increased damage, allowing it to hit multiple targets in a line.

Eyes Sharp

Buzz's Super range is increased significantly, allowing him to hook enemies from much farther distances.

Gadgets

Reserve Buoy

Buzz gains an instant second Super charge, allowing for consecutive hooks and eliminations.

X-ray-Vision

Buzz can see enemies through walls for a short duration, perfect for setting up hook ambushes.
