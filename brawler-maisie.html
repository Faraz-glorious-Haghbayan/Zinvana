Maisie is a precise sharpshooter who excels at long-range combat. Her unique attack mechanic allows her to deal massive damage to enemies at distance while maintaining excellent control over the battlefield. Perfect for players who love strategic positioning and precise aiming.

Abilities

Attack: Finish Them!

Maisie shoots a powerful projectile that deals more damage to enemies with lower health. The farther the enemy, the more damage it deals.

Damage: 1260 Range: Very Long Reload: 2.0s

Super: Boom Box

Maisie throws a boom box that bounces off walls and explodes when it hits an enemy or after a certain time, dealing area damage.

Damage: 1800 Range: Long Area: Medium

Star Powers

Tremors

Maisie's Super creates additional shockwaves that deal damage to enemies in a larger area around the explosion.

Legendary

Pinpoint Precision

Maisie's attacks deal 25% more damage to enemies below 50% health.

Legendary

Gadgets

Scope Out

Maisie's next attack has increased range and reveals enemies in bushes along its path.

Uses: 3

Disengage

Maisie quickly dashes backwards and gains a shield that absorbs the next 2000 damage.

Uses: 3
