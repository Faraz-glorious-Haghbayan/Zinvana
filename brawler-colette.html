Colette

The Enthusiastic Gift Shop Worker

Assassin Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Colette is an enthusiastic gift shop worker who deals damage based on enemy health percentages rather than fixed amounts. This unique assassin excels at taking down high-health targets with her percentage-based attacks that scale with enemy health. Her Super allows her to dash through enemies and return to her starting position, making her excellent at hit-and-run tactics. Colette's distinctive damage mechanics make her particularly effective against heavyweight brawlers and tanks.

Abilities

Attack: Tax Collection

Main Attack

Colette fires a projectile that deals damage equal to a percentage of the enemy's current health. More health = more damage taken.

Health damage: 37%
Min damage: 500
Range: 7.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Time to Collect

Super Ability

Colette dashes forward, dealing percentage-based damage to all enemies in her path, then returns to her starting position after a short delay.

Health damage: 20%
Min damage: 1,000
Range: 7 tiles
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Push It

Colette's attack pushes enemies away from her, providing knockback that can help with positioning and crowd control.

Mass Tax

Colette's Super charges 20% faster, allowing her to use her dash ability more frequently for better mobility and damage output.

Gadgets

Na-ah!

Colette instantly returns to her starting position and becomes invulnerable for 1 second. Perfect for escaping dangerous situations.

Gotcha!

Colette's next attack has 25% longer range and slows the target, making it easier to secure eliminations or escape.
