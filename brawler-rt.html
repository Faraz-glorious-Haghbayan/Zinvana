R-T

The Radar Robot

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
5,000 Health

Overview

R-T is an advanced robotic support unit equipped with sophisticated radar technology and team coordination systems. This mechanical marvel excels at providing battlefield intelligence and tactical support through his radar-based abilities. R-T's primary function is to enhance team positioning and awareness while providing defensive support through his technological superiority. His combination of radar detection, shield generation, and strategic positioning makes him an invaluable asset for coordinated team play and objective control.

Abilities

Attack: Radar Pulse

Main Attack

R-T emits a radar pulse that deals damage to enemies and reveals their positions to teammates for a short duration.

Damage: 840
Reveal duration: 3.0s
Range: 8.0
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Radar Station

Super Ability

R-T deploys a radar station that continuously reveals enemies in a large area and provides defensive shields to nearby allies.

Station health: 3,500
Reveal radius: 8.0
Shield per ally: 1,200
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Enhanced Radar

R-T's radar effects last 50% longer and can reveal enemies through walls, providing superior battlefield awareness.

Shield Protocol

R-T automatically generates a shield for the lowest health ally within his range every 5 seconds during combat.

Gadgets

EMP Blast

R-T releases an electromagnetic pulse that disables enemy abilities and gadgets in a large area around him.

Emergency Beacon

R-T instantly teleports to the location of his radar station, allowing for quick repositioning and defensive plays.
