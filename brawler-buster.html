Buster

The Stunt Performer

Tank Type
Mythic Rarity
7,000 Health

Overview

Buster is a fearless stunt performer who combines spectacular defensive capabilities with crowd control mechanics. This daredevil tank excels at protecting teammates and disrupting enemy formations through his unique shield-based abilities. Buster's theatrical background gives him the confidence to charge into danger while providing cover for his allies. His combination of high health, shield generation, and area control makes him an ideal frontline brawler who can absorb damage while setting up plays for his team.

Abilities

Attack: Stunt Double

Main Attack

Buster fires a projectile that creates a temporary shield barrier on impact, providing cover for himself and allies while dealing damage.

Damage: 1,200
Shield health: 2,000
Range: 7.33
Reload: 2.2s

Super: Blockbuster

Super Ability

Buster charges forward, stunning enemies in his path and creating a massive shield wall that blocks projectiles and provides team protection.

Stun damage: 1,600
Stun duration: 2.0s
Shield wall health: 4,000
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Blockbuster

Buster's shield barriers last 25% longer and provide additional protection, enhancing his defensive capabilities for extended engagements.

Stunt Double

When Buster's shield is destroyed, it explodes dealing area damage to nearby enemies and potentially eliminating weakened opponents.

Gadgets

Slo-Mo Replay

Buster slows down all enemies in a large area around him, making them easier targets for his team and reducing their escape potential.

Utility Belt

Buster instantly creates three shield barriers in a fan pattern, providing immediate cover and area control for strategic positioning.

Strategy & Tips

🛡️

Shield Placement

Position your shield barriers strategically to provide cover for teammates and control key battlefield areas.

💥

Charge Timing

Use Blockbuster when enemies are grouped together to maximize stun potential and shield wall effectiveness.

⏰

Extended Defense

With Blockbuster star power, use your longer-lasting shields to control areas for extended periods.

💣

Explosive Shields

Position Stunt Double shields near enemies so they take damage when the barriers are destroyed.

🐌

Crowd Control

Use Slo-Mo Replay to set up easy eliminations for your team or to escape dangerous situations.

🎯

Triple Shield

Deploy Utility Belt's three shields to instantly control chokepoints and create multiple defensive positions.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for protecting the gem carrier with shield barriers and controlling the central area.

Siege

Perfect for defending the IKE turret and creating protective barriers during robot construction.

Heist

Great for both defending the safe with shields and pushing toward the enemy safe with protective cover.
