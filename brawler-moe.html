Moe

The Underground Driller

Damage Dealer Type
Mythic Rarity
5,200 Health

Overview

Moe is a powerful damage dealer who brings underground drilling expertise to the battlefield through his massive drill and earth-moving abilities. This subterranean specialist excels at breaking through enemy defenses, controlling ground-based areas, and dealing massive damage with his industrial-grade equipment. Moe's gameplay revolves around positioning, timing his drilling attacks, and using his underground knowledge to control the battlefield. His construction background allows him to reshape the terrain and create strategic advantages for his team. Moe combines raw power with tactical excavation skills to become a formidable damage dealer who can break through any defense.

Abilities

Attack: Drill Strike

Main Attack

Moe swings his massive drill in a wide arc, dealing high damage to all enemies in front of him and destroying any walls or obstacles.

Damage: 920
Range: 4.0
Reload: 2.0s
Wall destruction: 100%

Super: Underground Tunnel

Super Ability

Moe digs underground and emerges at a target location, dealing damage to all enemies in the area and creating a tunnel that allies can use.

Emergence damage: 1,600
Tunnel duration: 8 seconds
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Seismic Shock

Moe's drill strikes create shockwaves that travel along the ground, dealing 50% damage to enemies behind the initial targets.

Tunnel Vision

Allies using Moe's tunnels gain increased movement speed and damage resistance for 3 seconds after emerging.

Gadgets

Drill Boost

Moe's next three attacks have increased range and pierce through all enemies, dealing full damage to each target.

Ground Pound

Moe slams his drill into the ground, creating a large shockwave that damages and slows all nearby enemies.
