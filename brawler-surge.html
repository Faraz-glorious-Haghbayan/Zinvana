Surge

The Protector of Starr Park

Fighter Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Surge is the enthusiastic protector of Starr Park who becomes stronger throughout each match with his unique upgrade system. This energetic fighter starts each game relatively weak but can upgrade twice during a match, gaining new abilities and increased power. His Super allows him to teleport and deal area damage, while his upgrade system transforms him from a basic fighter into a formidable late-game powerhouse. Surge rewards aggressive play and careful upgrade management.

Abilities

Attack: Juice of Justice

Main Attack

Surge fires a projectile that splits into multiple smaller projectiles upon hitting an enemy or reaching maximum range. Upgrades improve this attack.

Base damage: 1,260
Split damage: 420 each
Range: 8.33
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Party Tricks

Super Ability

Surge teleports to target location and deals area damage upon landing. Using the Super also upgrades Surge to his next stage (max 2 upgrades).

Damage: 1,680
Range: 8 tiles
Area: 2.67 radius
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

To the Max!

When Surge is at maximum upgrade level, his attack splits into 6 projectiles instead of the normal amount, dealing massive area damage.

Serve Ice Cold

When Surge is upgraded to Stage 2 or higher, his attack slows down enemies by 50% for 3 seconds, providing excellent crowd control.

Gadgets

Power Surge

Surge instantly upgrades to the next stage or, if already at max, resets his Super charge to zero but gains a shield for 3 seconds.

Power Shield

Surge gains a shield that absorbs up to 2,000 damage for 5 seconds, making him much more survivable during fights.

Strategy & Tips

⬆️

Upgrade Priority

Focus on charging your Super early to get your first upgrade. Each upgrade significantly increases your combat effectiveness.

📍

Teleport Positioning

Use your Super teleport strategically for escapes, gap closing, or surprising enemies. The area damage can finish low-health enemies.

💥

Split Shot Mastery

Position your attacks to hit multiple enemies with split projectiles. Higher upgrades create more splits for better area control.

❄️

Slow Control

With Serve Ice Cold and upgrades, your attacks become powerful crowd control tools. Use slows to help teammates.

🛡️

Shield Usage

Use Power Shield when engaging multiple enemies or when you need to secure an upgrade. Timing is crucial for survival.

⚡

Late Game Power

You become incredibly powerful at max upgrades. Play aggressively in the late game to maximize your potential.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Perfect for powering up throughout the match. His upgrade system makes him stronger as the game progresses.

Gem Grab

Great for controlling the center area once upgraded. Can teleport to safety or onto enemies strategically.

Bounty

Excellent mobility with teleport for getting picks and escaping danger. Split shots help control lanes.
