Poco

The Undead Mariachi Musician

Support Type
Trophy Road Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Poco is a unique undead mariachi musician who brings both musical flair and supernatural support to the battlefield. As a support brawler, Poco excels at healing teammates and dealing area damage with his enchanting melodies. His undead nature makes him resilient, while his musical abilities make him an invaluable team player.

Abilities

Attack: Bone-A-Fide

Main Attack

Poco strums his guitar, sending out musical notes in a wide cone that can hit multiple enemies. The attack has a decent range and can pierce through enemies.

Damage: 980
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.5s

Super: Encore

Super Ability

Poco plays an inspiring melody that heals himself and all nearby teammates. This ability has a massive area of effect and can turn the tide of battle.

Healing: 1,400
Range: 8.67
Charges: 5 hits

Star Powers

Da Capo!

Poco's Super also heals teammates by an additional 700 health over time. This makes Poco an even more effective healer and support brawler.

Screeching Solo

Poco's Super now also damages enemies for 800 damage. This transforms his healing ability into a versatile tool that can both heal allies and harm enemies.

Gadgets

Tuning Fork

Poco creates a protective totem that reduces damage taken by him and nearby teammates by 20%. The totem lasts for 6 seconds.

Protective Tunes

Poco immediately heals himself and nearby teammates for 1,000 health. This provides instant healing in critical moments.
