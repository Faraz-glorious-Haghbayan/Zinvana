Gray

The Portal Master

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
4,400 Health

Overview

Gray is a mysterious portal master who manipulates dimensional space to provide unprecedented team mobility and strategic advantages. This enigmatic brawler excels at creating shortcuts, enabling flanking maneuvers, and repositioning teammates through his portal-based abilities. Gray's mastery of dimensional magic allows him to bend the rules of battlefield positioning, creating new tactical possibilities for his team. His combination of mobility support, strategic positioning, and dimensional manipulation makes him an invaluable asset for coordinated team play.

Abilities

Attack: Dimensional Rift

Main Attack

Gray creates a dimensional rift that deals damage to enemies and temporarily weakens the fabric of space around them.

Damage: 980
Weaken effect: -15% damage
Range: 7.67
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Portal Network

Super Ability

Gray creates two connected portals that allow allies to instantly teleport between them, providing unparalleled team mobility.

Portal duration: 10s
Teleport range: Global
Cooldown: 2s per use
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Dimensional Anchor

Gray's portals last 30% longer and allies using them gain a temporary shield that absorbs damage for enhanced survivability.

Rift Walker

Gray can use his own portals and gains increased movement speed for 3 seconds after teleporting through any portal.

Gadgets

Phase Shift

Gray instantly teleports to a nearby location, allowing for quick repositioning or escaping from dangerous situations.

Portal Trap

Gray creates a trap portal that teleports the first enemy who steps on it to a random location on the battlefield.

Strategy & Tips

🌀

Portal Placement

Position portals strategically to create shortcuts to objectives or escape routes from dangerous areas.

🚀

Team Mobility

Coordinate with teammates to maximize portal usage for flanking maneuvers and surprise attacks.

🛡️

Shield Support

With Dimensional Anchor, ensure teammates use portals before engaging for the protective shield benefit.

⚡

Personal Mobility

Use Rift Walker to become highly mobile yourself, using portals for quick repositioning and speed boosts.

🌟

Quick Escape

Use Phase Shift to instantly escape from dangerous situations or reposition for better angles.

🪤

Trap Positioning

Place Portal Traps on objectives or chokepoints to disrupt enemy positioning and create chaos.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for providing gem carrier escapes and enabling quick rotations between defensive and offensive positions.

Siege

Perfect for enabling quick bolt collection and providing robot escort support through strategic portal placement.

Heist

Great for creating flanking routes to the enemy safe and providing quick escapes after successful attacks.
