Angelo

The Sky Hunter

Assassin Type
Mythic Rarity
4,000 Health

Overview

Angelo is a unique aerial assassin who dominates the battlefield from above with his flight capabilities and devastating dive attacks. This celestial warrior combines mobility, stealth, and precision to eliminate targets with surgical strikes from unexpected angles. Angelo's gameplay revolves around aerial positioning, timing dive attacks, and utilizing his flight abilities to outmaneuver enemies. His heavenly powers allow him to soar above the battlefield, making him nearly untouchable while positioning for the perfect strike. Angelo excels at hit-and-run tactics, using his aerial advantage to engage and disengage at will, making him a formidable assassin who strikes fear into enemies below.

Abilities

Attack: Celestial Bolts

Main Attack

Angelo fires bolts of celestial energy that pierce through enemies and deal increased damage when fired from above.

Damage per bolt: 720
Bolts per shot: 3
Aerial bonus: +25%
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.7s

Super: Divine Dive

Super Ability

Angelo launches into the air and can target an area to dive down, dealing massive damage to enemies in the impact zone.

Dive damage: 3,200
Impact radius: 3 tiles
Flight time: 3 seconds
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Heavenly Grace

Angelo becomes temporarily invincible for 1 second after landing his Divine Dive, allowing for safe repositioning or follow-up attacks.

Aerial Supremacy

Angelo can fly over walls and obstacles during his normal movement, gaining 20% increased movement speed while flying.

Gadgets

Wing Gust

Angelo creates a powerful wind gust that knocks back all nearby enemies and makes him immune to crowd control for 3 seconds.

Halo Shield

Angelo creates a protective halo that absorbs the next 2500 damage and reflects 50% of absorbed damage back to attackers.

Strategy & Tips

🦅

Aerial Advantage

Use your flight ability to position above enemies for maximum damage output and strategic advantage.

🎯

Dive Timing

Time your Divine Dive carefully to catch enemies off-guard or escape dangerous situations.

✨

Grace Period

With Heavenly Grace, use your invincibility window to secure eliminations or reposition safely.

🌪️

Mobility Master

Use Aerial Supremacy to fly over walls and access flanking routes that other brawlers can't reach.

💨

Crowd Control

Use Wing Gust to escape teamfights or push enemies into unfavorable positions.

🛡️

Defensive Halo

Activate Halo Shield before engaging in combat to absorb damage and reflect it back to enemies.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Perfect for utilizing aerial mobility to third-party fights and escape dangerous situations.

Bounty

Excellent for picking off high-value targets with dive attacks and escaping quickly.

Knockout

Great for eliminating key enemies and using flight to access unexpected flanking routes.
