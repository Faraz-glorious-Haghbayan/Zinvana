Spike

The Loveable Cactus

Damage Dealer Type
Mythic Rarity
3,600 Health

Overview

Spike is a loveable cactus who doesn't say much but lets his spines do the talking. This mythic brawler excels at area control and burst damage, making him one of the most versatile characters in the game. His unique attack pattern and crowd control Super make him effective in almost any situation, earning him a place among the top-tier brawlers.

Abilities

Attack: Needle Grenade

Main Attack

Spike throws a cactus grenade that explodes on impact, shooting spikes in all directions. The main projectile deals significant damage, while the spikes provide excellent area control.

Main damage: 896
Spike damage: 448 each
Range: 8.67
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Stick Around!

Super Ability

Spike throws a large cactus grenade that creates a field of spikes on the ground. Enemies who walk through take damage and are slowed down significantly.

Initial damage: 448
Field damage: 560 per second
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Fertilize

Spike heals 800 health per second while inside his Super's area of effect. This allows him to control territory while staying healthy.

Curveball

Spike's attack spikes curve around walls and obstacles, making it much harder for enemies to hide behind cover.

Gadgets

Popping Pincushion

Spike instantly shoots spikes in all directions around himself, dealing 520 damage. Perfect for close-range emergencies or finishing off low-health enemies.

Life Plant

Spike grows a healing cactus that heals him and nearby allies for 800 health per second for 5 seconds. Great for team support and sustain.
