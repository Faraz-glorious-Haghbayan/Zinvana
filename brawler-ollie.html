Ollie

The Graffiti Artist

Damage Dealer Type
Mythic Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Ollie is a creative graffiti artist who brings urban style and incredible mobility to the battlefield. This mythic brawler has a unique dual-mode system - he can fight on foot with spray paint attacks or transform into his skateboard for enhanced speed and different attack patterns. His ability to switch between modes makes him one of the most versatile and unpredictable brawlers in the game, perfect for players who love dynamic, fast-paced gameplay.

Abilities

Attack: Spray Tag

Main Attack (Human Mode)

Ollie sprays a burst of colorful paint that deals damage over time to enemies caught in the cloud. The paint lingers briefly, creating area denial zones that discourage enemies from pushing.

Initial damage: 640
DoT damage: 320 per second
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.6s

Attack: Kickflip Strike

Main Attack (Skateboard Mode)

While riding his skateboard, Ollie performs kickflip attacks that deal burst damage in a cone. The attacks are faster but have shorter range, perfect for aggressive close-quarters combat.

Damage: 880
Attack speed: Fast
Range: 4.33
Movement boost: +25%

Super: Mode Switch

Super Ability

Ollie transforms between human and skateboard mode. Switching modes grants a brief speed boost and resets his attack pattern, allowing for combo plays and tactical repositioning.

Switch speed boost: +40% for 2s
Charges: 4 hits

Star Powers

Fresh Coat

Ollie's spray paint attacks leave trails that slow enemies by 15% for 2 seconds. This makes it harder for enemies to escape his damage zones and helps control enemy movement.

Rail Grind

While in skateboard mode, Ollie gains a shield that absorbs 600 damage. The shield regenerates after being out of combat for 5 seconds, making him more durable during aggressive plays.

Gadgets

Tag Wall

Ollie sprays a temporary wall of graffiti that blocks enemy movement and projectiles for 3 seconds. Perfect for creating cover or blocking enemy advances.

Ollie Flip

Ollie performs a trick jump that makes him invulnerable for 0.8 seconds and deals 400 damage when landing. Great for dodging attacks or finishing off low-health enemies.
