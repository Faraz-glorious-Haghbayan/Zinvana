Darryl

The Rolling Robot

Tank Type
Super Rare Rarity
6,800 Health

Overview

Darryl is a mechanical pirate tank who rolls into battle with devastating close-range shotgun attacks and incredible mobility. This barrel-shaped robot can charge across the battlefield with his Super, breaking through enemy lines and dealing massive damage up close. His unique combination of high health, mobility, and burst damage makes him a formidable opponent who can quickly turn the tide of battle. Darryl excels at surprising enemies and eliminating key targets.

Abilities

Attack: Double Deuce

Main Attack

Darryl fires two shotgun shells that spread out and deal more damage at close range. Perfect for devastating enemies who get too close to this mechanical pirate.

Max Damage: 2,380
Min Damage: 1,428
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Barrel Roll

Super Ability

Darryl rolls forward in his barrel, knocking back enemies and dealing damage. This provides incredible mobility and allows him to close gaps or escape danger instantly.

Damage: 1,400
Range: 8.67
Shield Duration: 0.9s
Charges: Auto-charge

Star Powers

Steel Hoops

Darryl takes 90% less damage for 0.9 seconds after using his Super. This makes him nearly invincible during and right after his roll.

Rolling Reload

Darryl reloads 100% faster for 5 seconds after using his Super. This allows for devastating follow-up attacks after rolling in.

Gadgets

Recoiling Rotator

Darryl spins around and knocks back all nearby enemies while dealing 1,000 damage. Perfect for crowd control in tight situations.

Tar Barrel

Darryl leaves a puddle of tar behind him that slows enemies by 50% for 5 seconds. Great for controlling enemy movement.
