Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed answers about how our quizzes work, what results mean, and how to use them for self-discovery.

How do I start a test?+

Simply click on the "Test" link in the top navigation or choose from our available quizzes. You'll be presented with a series of multiple-choice questions. Select your answer and continue until you reach the end.

How long does a test take?+

Our streamlined assessments typically take under five minutes to complete. Each question is carefully designed to be concise and intuitive, ensuring an efficient yet comprehensive evaluation of your personality traits.

What do my results mean?+

At the end, you'll see your dominant personality type or archetype. You'll also get a summary of strengths, areas for improvement, and recommended insights. These are meant for self-discovery and general guidance.

Are my results stored or shared?+

Absolutely not. Zinvana operates with a strict privacy-first approach. We do not store, track, or share any personal data or individual results. Your responses are processed in real-time for immediate analysis and then permanently discarded.

How accurate are the tests?+

Our assessments are grounded in established psychological frameworks and validated methodologies. While no personality test can capture the full complexity of human nature, our results provide valuable insights as a foundation for self-reflection and personal understanding.

How do I restart a test?+

Click the "Restart Test" button on the results page or refresh your browser. The test will reset, and you can begin again from question one.

How do I share my results?+

After completing the test, use the share buttons to copy a link or share directly to social media. You can also save an image of your results for personal use.
