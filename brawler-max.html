Max

The Super Speedy Support

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
3,800 Health

Overview

Max is an energetic superhero who brings speed and energy to any team composition. This high-mobility support brawler excels at boosting her teammates' movement speed while dealing consistent damage with her energy drink blasters. Her Super ability provides incredible team utility by increasing everyone's speed, making rotations faster and enabling aggressive plays. Max's combination of mobility, team support, and decent damage output makes her a versatile pick in various game modes.

Abilities

Attack: Phase Blaster

Main Attack

Max fires four energy projectiles in a long range that deal damage to enemies. The projectiles have a wide spread and can hit multiple targets.

Damage per projectile: 320
Max damage: 1,280
Range: 9.33
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Let's Go!

Super Ability

Max boosts her movement speed and the movement speed of all nearby teammates for a duration. This speed boost affects Max herself and allies within range.

Speed Boost: +35%
Duration: 4s
Range: 8.0
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Super Charged

Max's movement speed is increased by 25% for the entire match. This permanent speed boost makes Max even more mobile and harder to catch.

Run n Gun

Max reloads 25% faster when moving. This star power synergizes perfectly with her high mobility, allowing for more aggressive playstyles.

Gadgets

Phase Shifter

Max becomes immune to all damage and effects for 1 second but cannot attack during this time. Perfect for escaping dangerous situations or avoiding burst damage.

Sneaky Sneakers

Max becomes invisible for 2 seconds. During this time, she can move freely but cannot attack. Great for repositioning or flanking enemies.
