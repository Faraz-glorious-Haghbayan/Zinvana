Chuck

The Speed Demon

Damage Dealer Type
Epic Rarity
4,480 Health

Overview

Chuck is a high-mobility damage dealer who excels at hit-and-run tactics and quick repositioning. His speed-based abilities allow him to quickly engage enemies, deal damage, and escape before they can retaliate. Chuck's playstyle revolves around constant movement and positioning, making him a challenging but rewarding brawler to master. His ability to control engagements through superior mobility makes him a nightmare for slower opponents.

Abilities

Attack: Speed Shot

Main Attack

Chuck fires a rapid projectile that deals good damage. The projectile travels quickly and has decent range, perfect for hit-and-run tactics.

Damage: 1,120
Range: 7
Reload: 1.3s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Lightning Rush

Super Ability

Chuck dashes forward at incredible speed, dealing damage to all enemies in his path. He gains a speed boost for a short time after using his Super.

Damage: 1,680
Speed boost: +50%
Charges: 4 hits

Star Powers

Turbo Charge

Chuck gains a 25% speed boost for 3 seconds after eliminating an enemy. Perfect for chaining eliminations and maintaining momentum.

Afterburner

Chuck's Super leaves a trail of fire that deals 300 damage per second to enemies who walk through it. Great for area denial.

Gadgets

Nitro Boost

Chuck gains a massive speed boost and immunity to slowing effects for 4 seconds. Perfect for escaping dangerous situations.

Rapid Fire

Chuck's next three attacks reload instantly and have 15% increased damage. Great for burst damage opportunities.

Strategy & Tips

⚡

Mobility Master

Use your superior speed to control engagements. Engage when you have the advantage and retreat when you don't.

🎯

Hit and Run

Attack enemies and immediately move to a new position. Don't stand still and trade damage with opponents.

🌪️

Super Positioning

Use your Super to dash through multiple enemies or escape dangerous situations. The speed boost is crucial for survival.

🎪

Unpredictable Movement

Change direction frequently and use unpredictable movement patterns to avoid enemy attacks.

📍

Map Awareness

Know all the escape routes and positioning opportunities. Your mobility is only as good as your map knowledge.

🔄

Constant Pressure

Keep enemies on their toes with constant harassment. Your speed allows you to pressure multiple enemies.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Perfect for solo play with your mobility allowing you to control engagements and escape dangerous situations.

Gem Grab

Excellent for harassing enemies and supporting your gem carrier with your speed and damage output.

Brawl Ball

Great for carrying the ball and scoring goals with your speed, plus pressuring enemy ball carriers.
