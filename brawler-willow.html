Willow

The Mind Controller

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
4,400 Health

Overview

Willow is a mysterious psychic brawler who manipulates minds and controls enemy actions through her supernatural abilities. This enigmatic support specialist excels at disrupting enemy strategies by taking control of their brawlers and turning them against their own team. Willow's mind control powers allow her to create chaos in enemy ranks while providing unique tactical advantages for her allies. Her combination of psychic manipulation, crowd control, and strategic disruption makes her one of the most unpredictable and powerful support brawlers in the game.

Abilities

Attack: Psychic Blast

Main Attack

Willow fires a psychic projectile that deals damage and weakens enemy mental defenses, making them more susceptible to mind control.

Damage: 980
Weakness effect: 2.0s
Range: 7.67
Reload: 2.1s

Super: Mind Control

Super Ability

Willow takes complete control of an enemy brawler, allowing her to control their movement and attacks for a short duration.

Control duration: 3.0s
Control range: 8.0
Damage boost: 25%
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Dominating Presence

Willow's mind control lasts 50% longer and controlled enemies deal additional damage to their former allies.

Mental Fortress

Willow gains a shield that absorbs 30% of incoming damage and reflects 15% back to attackers through psychic energy.

Gadgets

Psychic Pulse

Willow releases a wave that stuns all enemies in a large area around her, creating opportunities for mind control.

Mental Link

Willow creates a psychic connection with an ally, allowing them to share damage and healing effects for mutual support.
