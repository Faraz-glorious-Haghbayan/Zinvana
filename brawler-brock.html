Brock

The Rocket Launcher

Sharpshooter Type
Trophy Road Rarity
2,800 Health

Overview

Brock is an explosive sharpshooter who loves big booms and even bigger damage numbers. Armed with a powerful rocket launcher, he excels at long-range combat and area control. His rockets can destroy walls and deal splash damage, making him excellent for controlling choke points and eliminating grouped enemies. His aggressive playstyle and high damage output make him a formidable threat at any range.

Abilities

Attack: Rockin' Rocket

Main Attack

Brock fires a rocket that explodes on impact, dealing area damage. The rocket has a long range and can destroy walls, making it perfect for both offense and utility.

Damage: 1,120
Range: 10.0
Reload: 1.8s
Projectile speed: 2000

Super: Rocket Rain

Super Ability

Brock fires a barrage of rockets over walls that rain down on a large area. This Super can hit enemies behind cover and deals massive area damage.

Damage per rocket: 560
Number of rockets: 9
Charges: 10 hits

Star Powers

Incendiary

Brock's attack sets the ground on fire for 2 seconds, dealing 300 damage per second to enemies who walk through it. Great for area denial.

Rocket No. 4

Brock's Super fires an additional rocket, increasing the total from 9 to 10 rockets. This provides more damage and better area coverage.

Gadgets

Rocket Laces

Brock blasts off into the air and lands at target location, dealing 1,000 damage to nearby enemies. Perfect for repositioning or finishing enemies.

Rocket Fuel

Brock's next attack has 50% longer range and 30% faster projectile speed. Great for catching distant enemies off guard.

Strategy & Tips

🎯

Predict Movement

Brock's rockets have travel time. Learn to predict enemy movement and lead your shots for maximum hit rate.

🧱

Break Cover

Use your rockets to destroy enemy walls and cover. This forces enemies into the open where they're easier to hit.

📍

Control Choke Points

Position yourself to control narrow passages and key map areas with your area damage and long range.

🌪️

Super Behind Cover

Use your Super to hit enemies hiding behind walls. The rockets go over obstacles and can surprise enemies.

🏃

Maintain Distance

Keep enemies at long range where your rockets are most effective. Avoid close-quarters combat whenever possible.

💥

Splash Damage

Aim for grouped enemies to maximize your splash damage. Even near misses can deal significant damage.

Best Game Modes

Heist

Excellent for destroying the enemy safe and breaking walls that protect it. His high damage is perfect for objectives.

Siege

Great for controlling the bolt and dealing massive damage to the IKE turret from a safe distance.

Bounty

Perfect for long-range eliminations and controlling key map areas with his superior range and damage.
