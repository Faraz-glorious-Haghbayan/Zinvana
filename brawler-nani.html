Nani

The Drone Controller

Sharpshooter Type
Epic Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Nani is a high-tech robot designed for precision warfare and remote surveillance. This advanced brawler excels at long-range combat and strategic drone deployment. Her unique ability to control a flying drone makes her incredibly versatile for both offensive strikes and reconnaissance missions. Nani's technological superiority rewards skilled positioning and precise aiming, making her a formidable opponent in the right hands.

Abilities

Attack: Trigger-Nometry

Main Attack

Nani shoots three light orbs that pierce through enemies and deal damage. The orbs travel in a straight line and can hit multiple targets.

Damage per orb: 644
Max damage: 1,932
Range: 11
Reload: 1.5s

Super: Manual Override

Super Ability

Nani takes control of Peep, her surveillance drone, and can fly it around the map to crash into enemies for massive damage.

Damage: 2,800
Duration: 3s
Speed: Fast
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Autofocus

Nani's main attack deals significantly more damage when all three orbs hit the same target, increasing damage by 50%.

Tempered Steel

Nani's Peep drone gains a shield and deals more damage the longer it travels, increasing damage by up to 80%.

Gadgets

Warp Blast

Nani instantly teleports to her Peep drone's location when it explodes. Great for repositioning and surprise attacks.

Return to Sender

Peep explodes instantly when activated, dealing damage in a larger area. Perfect for quick eliminations.

Strategy & Tips

🎯

Precision Aiming

Focus on hitting all three orbs on the same target to maximize damage output, especially with Autofocus star power.

🚁

Drone Scouting

Use Peep to scout enemy positions and gather intelligence before committing to attacks or rotations.

📡

Teleport Tactics

With Warp Blast, you can teleport to unexpected locations. Use this for escapes or aggressive repositioning.

🛡️

Distance Management

Stay at maximum range to avoid retaliation. Nani is fragile but deadly from afar.

⚡

Quick Elimination

Use Return to Sender for instant Peep explosions when enemies are low on health or grouped together.

🎮

Remote Control Master

Practice controlling Peep smoothly. Good drone control separates average Nani players from great ones.

Best Game Modes

Bounty

Excellent for long-range elimination and scouting. Peep can secure kills on low-health enemies hiding behind cover.

Siege

Great for controlling the robot and dealing damage from safe distances. Peep can help defend or attack the IKE.

Gem Grab

Effective at controlling lanes and providing vision. Can support teammates while staying safe in the back.
