Squeak

The Gooey Guardian

Support Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Squeak is a cheerful blob creature who specializes in sticky explosive warfare and battlefield control. His unique ability to attach explosive blobs to enemies and terrain makes him a formidable support brawler with both offensive and defensive capabilities. Squeak's gooey projectiles can stick to walls and enemies, creating delayed explosions that control areas and catch opponents off guard. His playful demeanor hides a tactical mind perfect for strategic gameplay.

Abilities

Attack: Sticky Blomb

Main Attack

Squeak launches a blob that sticks to enemies, walls, or the ground. After a short delay, the blob explodes dealing area damage.

Damage: 1,160
Explosion delay: 2s
Range: 7.67
Reload: 2.2s

Super: Big Blob

Super Ability

Squeak launches a massive blob that explodes into six smaller blobs upon impact, creating a large area of delayed explosions.

Impact damage: 1,000
Small blob damage: 700
Total blobs: 6
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Chain Reaction

When Squeak's blobs explode, they trigger a chain reaction that makes nearby blobs explode instantly, creating devastating combos.

Super Sticky

Squeak's blobs slow down enemies by 50% when they stick to them, making it easier to land follow-up attacks.

Gadgets

Windup

Squeak's next attack has reduced explosion delay, making the blob explode almost instantly for quick eliminations.

Residue

When Squeak takes damage, he automatically drops a blob at his location that explodes after a short delay.

Strategy & Tips

💣

Sticky Timing

Master the 2-second delay. Predict enemy movement to ensure your blobs explode when enemies are nearby.

🎯

Wall Bouncing

Use walls to reach enemies behind cover. Blobs stick to walls and can catch hiding opponents.

⚡

Chain Combos

With Chain Reaction, place multiple blobs near each other to create devastating chain explosions.

🛡️

Area Control

Use your Super to control large areas and force enemies into unfavorable positions.

🐌

Slow and Steady

With Super Sticky, slowed enemies are easy targets for teammates and follow-up attacks.

🔥

Quick Blast

Use Windup gadget for instant eliminations when enemies are low on health or surprised.

Best Game Modes

Siege

Excellent for controlling robot lanes and defending/attacking the IKE turret with area denial explosions.

Heist

Great for both attacking the safe and defending it. Blobs can stick to the safe and deal massive damage.

Hot Zone

Perfect for controlling zones with delayed explosions and forcing enemies out of contested areas.
