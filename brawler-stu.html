Stu

The Stunt Driver

Assassin Type
Chromatic Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Stu is a high-mobility assassin brawler who excels at quick engagements and escapes. His unique ability to charge his Super extremely fast makes him one of the most mobile brawlers in the game. Perfect for players who love hit-and-run tactics and aggressive plays that require precision timing and positioning.

Abilities

Attack: Razzle Dazzle

Main Attack

Stu fires two projectiles in quick succession that deal decent damage. The projectiles have a slight spread and work best at close to medium range for maximum effectiveness.

Damage: 1,260
Projectiles: 2
Range: 7.67
Reload: 1.7s

Super: Nitro Boost

Super Ability

Stu dashes forward at incredible speed, dealing damage and knocking back enemies. This Super charges extremely quickly - just one hit from his main attack is enough!

Damage: 1,120
Knockback: Yes
Charges: 1 hit

Star Powers

Zero Drag

Stu's next attack after using his Super will charge his Super. This creates an incredible mobility loop that allows for continuous dashing in team fights and extended engagements.

Gaso-Heal

Stu heals 800 health whenever he uses his Super. This provides much-needed sustainability for aggressive plays and helps him stay in fights longer.

Gadgets

Speed Zone

Stu drops a zone that increases the movement speed of him and his allies by 24% for 6 seconds. Perfect for team pushes, rotations, and escaping danger.

Breakthrough

Stu's next Super will pull enemies towards him instead of knocking them back. Great for isolating key targets or disrupting enemy formations.
