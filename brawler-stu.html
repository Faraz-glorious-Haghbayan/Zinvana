Stu is a high-mobility assassin brawler who excels at quick engagements and escapes. His unique ability to charge his Super extremely fast makes him one of the most mobile brawlers in the game. Perfect for players who love hit-and-run tactics and aggressive plays.

Abilities

Attack: Raging Fire

Stu shoots two short-range flames that deal decent damage. The attack has a wide spread and is perfect for close-range combat.

Damage: 1260 Range: Short Reload: 1.6s

Super: Nitro Boost

Stu dashes forward, dealing damage to enemies in his path. The Super charges incredibly fast, making Stu extremely mobile in battles.

Damage: 1800 Range: Long Charges: Fast

Star Powers

Zero Drag

Stu's next attack after using his Super will have longer range and faster projectile speed.

Legendary

Gaso-Heal
