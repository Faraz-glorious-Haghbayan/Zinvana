Leon

The Stealthy Assassin

Assassin Type
Legendary Rarity
3,600 Health

Overview

Leon is a legendary assassin who specializes in stealth and surprise attacks. With his unique invisibility ability and spinner-based attacks, he can appear out of nowhere to eliminate unsuspecting enemies. His ninja-like appearance and sneaky playstyle make him perfect for players who enjoy strategic positioning and timing their attacks perfectly.

Abilities

Attack: Spinner Blades

Main Attack

Leon throws a spinning blade that deals high damage up close. The blade passes through enemies and deals less damage the farther it travels, making Leon most effective at close range.

Close Damage: 1,596
Far Damage: 700
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.9s

Super: Smoke Bomb

Super Ability

Leon becomes invisible for 6 seconds, allowing him to sneak up on enemies or escape dangerous situations. The invisibility breaks when he attacks or gets too close to enemies.

Duration: 6 seconds
Detection Range: 4 tiles
Charges: 10 hits

Star Powers

Smoke Trails

Leon's movement speed increases by 30% while invisible and for 1 second after becoming visible again. This makes him incredibly mobile and harder to catch.

Invisiheal

Leon recovers 1,000 health per second while invisible. This makes his Super both an offensive and defensive tool, allowing him to heal while repositioning.

Gadgets

Smoke Bomb

Leon instantly becomes invisible for 1 second. This provides a quick escape or repositioning tool that doesn't require his full Super charge.

Lollipop Drop

Leon drops a lollipop that provides a 30% damage reduction shield for 3 seconds to himself and nearby teammates. Great for team support.
