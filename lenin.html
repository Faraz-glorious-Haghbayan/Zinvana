Vladimir Lenin

The Revolutionary Architect of the Soviet State

Born: April 22, 1870

Died: January 21, 1924

Nationality: Russian

Occupation: Revolutionary, Politician, Theorist

Notable For: Leader of the Bolshevik Revolution

Early Life and Education

Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov, known by his alias Lenin, was born into a middle-class family in Simbirsk (now Ulyanovsk), Russia. From a young age, he was deeply affected by the execution of his brother and the injustices of the Tsarist regime. Educated in the classics and influenced by revolutionary ideas, Lenin’s early experiences laid the groundwork for his later political activism.
