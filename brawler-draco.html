Draco

The Fire Dragon

Tank Type
Mythic Rarity
7,200 Health

Overview

Draco is a formidable tank brawler who combines the raw power of a dragon with heavy armor and devastating fire attacks. This draconic warrior excels at leading charges, absorbing damage, and controlling areas with his fire-based abilities. Draco's gameplay revolves around aggressive positioning, using his massive health pool to lead team advances, and punishing enemies with burning damage over time. His dragon heritage grants him unique abilities that make him both a fearsome opponent and an invaluable team asset. Draco thrives in close-quarters combat where his fire breath and draconic might can devastate multiple enemies while his armor allows him to survive concentrated fire from the enemy team.

Abilities

Attack: Dragon Breath

Main Attack

Draco breathes a cone of fire that deals damage to all enemies in front of him and applies a burning effect that deals damage over time.

Initial damage: 800
Burn damage: 200/sec
Burn duration: 4 seconds
Range: 5.33
Reload: 2.2s

Super: Inferno Charge

Super Ability

Draco charges forward with incredible speed, dealing damage to all enemies in his path and leaving a trail of fire behind him.

Charge damage: 1,400
Trail damage: 400/sec
Charge distance: 8 tiles
Trail duration: 5 seconds
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Dragon Scales

Draco's thick scales reduce all incoming damage by 20%, and he gains immunity to all crowd control effects for 2 seconds after taking damage.

Blazing Fury

Draco's burning effects spread to nearby enemies, and his attack speed increases by 25% for each enemy currently burning.

Gadgets

Flame Shield

Draco creates a protective barrier of fire that absorbs 3000 damage and damages any enemy who attacks him for 6 seconds.

Meteor Strike

Draco calls down a meteor that strikes the target area after 2 seconds, dealing massive damage and creating a burning zone.
