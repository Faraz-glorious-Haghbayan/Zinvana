8-Bit

The Retro Arcade Fighter

Fighter Type
Epic Rarity
8,800 Health

Overview

8-Bit is a retro arcade fighter who deals massive damage with his laser blaster but moves very slowly. This pixelated powerhouse excels at long-range combat and can boost his team's damage with his Super turret. Despite his slow movement speed, 8-Bit's high health and incredible damage output make him a formidable force on the battlefield. His unique playstyle rewards positioning and teamwork, making him perfect for players who prefer calculated, high-impact gameplay.

Abilities

Attack: Blaster Beams

Main Attack

8-Bit shoots a spread of laser beams that deal heavy damage to enemies. The beams have a wide spread but high damage potential.

Damage: 448 per beam
Max damage: 2,688
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.5s

Super: Damage Booster

Super Ability

8-Bit deploys a damage booster turret that increases the damage of all friendly brawlers within its area of effect by 50%.

Damage boost: 50%
Duration: 20 seconds
Range: 5.33
Health: 3,000

Star Powers

Boosted Booster

8-Bit's damage booster turret now also increases the movement speed of friendly brawlers within its range by 20%.

Plugged In

When 8-Bit is within range of his damage booster, he gains increased movement speed, helping offset his natural slowness.

Gadgets

Cheat Cartridge

8-Bit instantly teleports to his damage booster turret, allowing for quick repositioning and escapes from dangerous situations.

Extra Credits

8-Bit's next attack deals 40% more damage and destroys obstacles, perfect for breaking through enemy cover or dealing finishing blows.
