Meg

The Mech Pilot

Fighter Type
Legendary Rarity
3,920 Health

Overview

Meg is a unique legendary fighter who transforms from a small, agile pilot into a massive, powerful mech suit during battle. Her dual-form gameplay makes her one of the most dynamic brawlers in the game, offering completely different playstyles in each form. As a pilot, she's quick and evasive, but once she transforms into her mech, she becomes a devastating powerhouse. Her transformation mechanic adds incredible strategic depth to her gameplay.

Abilities

Attack: Pew Pew / Heavy Metal

Main Attack

As a pilot, Meg fires rapid energy shots. As a mech, she launches heavy missiles that deal massive area damage. The transformation completely changes her combat style.

Pilot damage: 560
Mech damage: 1,400
Pilot range: 7
Mech range: 8.5

Super: Mech Deploy

Super Ability

Meg transforms into her powerful mech suit, gaining massive health, increased damage, and area attack capabilities. The mech has its own health pool and abilities.

Mech health: 7,000
Transformation time: 1.5s
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Force Field

When Meg transforms into her mech, she gains a 1,500 health shield that absorbs damage. The shield regenerates when she's out of combat.

Self-Destruction

When Meg's mech is destroyed, it explodes dealing 2,000 damage to all nearby enemies. Great for posthumous eliminations.

Gadgets

Jolting Volts

Meg's mech releases an electric pulse that deals 1,200 damage to all nearby enemies and stuns them for 1 second.

Mech Repair

Meg's mech instantly repairs 2,500 health and gains immunity to all debuffs for 3 seconds. Perfect for extending mech time.
