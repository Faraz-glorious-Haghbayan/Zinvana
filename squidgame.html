What is Squid Game?

Unless you've been living under a rock, you've probably heard of Squid Game — the global Netflix phenomenon that took the world by storm. Set in South Korea, the series revolves around a deadly competition where financially struggling individuals compete in childhood games for a chance at a massive cash prize. But there's a dark twist: losing means elimination — literally.

Beyond the shocking visuals and gripping plot, Squid Game dives deep into human psychology, survival instincts, and moral dilemmas. Every character represents a unique personality type, making it a perfect backdrop for exploring behavior, choices, and identity. That’s exactly why we built this test — to help you discover which Squid Game character best matches your personality.

Are you the mastermind strategist, the selfless leader, the loyal follower — or something more unexpected? Take the test and find out.
