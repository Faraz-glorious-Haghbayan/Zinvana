Shade

The Shadow Walker

Assassin Type
Legendary Rarity
3,600 Health

Overview

Shade is a master assassin who manipulates shadows and darkness to eliminate enemies with surgical precision. This enigmatic warrior excels at stealth gameplay, shadow manipulation, and striking from the darkness when enemies least expect it. Shade's gameplay revolves around positioning, timing, and using the shadows to gain tactical advantages. His mastery of darkness allows him to become nearly invisible and strike with deadly efficiency. Shade combines stealth, mobility, and precision to become the ultimate assassin who can eliminate high-priority targets before disappearing back into the shadows.

Abilities

Attack: Shadow Strike

Main Attack

Shade throws shadow blades that deal increased damage when attacking from stealth and reduce enemy vision temporarily.

Damage: 780
Range: 6.0
Reload: 1.4s
Stealth bonus: +50%

Super: Shadow Realm

Super Ability

Shade enters the shadow realm, becoming invisible and gaining increased movement speed while leaving shadow copies that confuse enemies.

Duration: 4 seconds
Speed boost: 40%
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Shadow Clone

Shade creates a shadow clone when taking damage that mimics his movements for 3 seconds and explodes when destroyed or expired.

Darkness Spread

Shade's attacks create zones of darkness that reduce enemy vision and movement speed by 25% for 4 seconds.

Gadgets

Shadow Dash

Shade instantly dashes through enemies, dealing damage and becoming invisible for 2 seconds while gaining a shield.

Nightmare

Shade creates a dark zone that fears enemies, forcing them to walk away from the center while taking damage over time.

Strategy & Tips

🌑

Stealth Positioning

Use shadows and cover to remain undetected before striking with your enhanced stealth damage.

⚔️

Assassinate Priority

Focus on eliminating high-value targets like enemy damage dealers and support brawlers first.

👥

Clone Confusion

With Shadow Clone, use your duplicates to confuse enemies and create distractions during fights.

🌫️

Vision Control

Use Darkness Spread to control enemy vision and create areas where you have a tactical advantage.

💨

Dash Escape

Use Shadow Dash to quickly engage enemies or escape dangerous situations while gaining defensive benefits.

😱

Fear Control

Use Nightmare to control enemy positioning and force them into unfavorable situations.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Perfect for eliminating isolated enemies and using stealth to avoid unwanted confrontations.

Bounty

Excellent for picking off high-value targets and escaping before enemy reinforcements arrive.

Gem Grab

Great for eliminating the enemy gem carrier and creating chaos in enemy formations.
