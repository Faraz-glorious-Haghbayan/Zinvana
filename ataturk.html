Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

The Visionary Reformer and Founder of Modern Turkey

Born: May 19, 1881

Died: November 10, 1938

Nationality: Turkish

Occupation: Military Officer, Statesman

Notable For: Founding Modern Turkey and implementing sweeping reforms

Early Life and Education

Mustafa Kemal Atatürk was born in Salonica (now Thessaloniki, Greece) in 1881. Raised in a multicultural environment, he received a modern education that laid the groundwork for his later military and political career. His early exposure to various cultures and ideas inspired his vision for a progressive, secular, and modern Turkey.
