The Tech Educator

From Computer Store to Tech Empire

Linus Sebastian transformed from a computer store employee in Canada to the most influential technology educator on the internet. Starting with simple product videos, he built Linus Tech Tips into a multimedia empire that includes multiple YouTube channels, a successful merchandise line, and industry-changing initiatives.

His approach to technology reviews combines deep technical knowledge with accessible explanations, making complex topics understandable for both beginners and experts. Linus has become the go-to source for honest hardware reviews, PC building guides, and tech industry insights.

Beyond individual success, he's revolutionized how technology is presented online, emphasizing transparency, detailed testing methodologies, and community engagement. His team at Linus Media Group continues to push boundaries in tech content creation and education.
