Nita

The Nature Warrior

Fighter Type
Rare Rarity
5,200 Health

Overview

Nita is a fierce young warrior who commands the power of nature and her trusty bear companion. With her shockwave attacks and ability to summon a powerful bear ally, she excels at both offense and area control. Nita's versatile playstyle allows her to adapt to various situations, making her an excellent choice for players who enjoy having a loyal companion in battle. Her connection with nature gives her unique tactical advantages on the battlefield.

Abilities

Attack: Rupture

Main Attack

Nita sends forward a shockwave that ripples through the ground, dealing damage to all enemies in its path. The attack has a wide spread, making it effective against multiple targets.

Damage: 1,300
Range: 7.67
Reload: 1.25s
Projectile Speed: 2000

Super: Overbearing

Super Ability

Nita summons a big, bad bear that will hunt down enemies and attack them with fierce swipes. The bear acts as an independent ally that can tank damage and pressure enemies.

Bear Health: 4,200
Bear Damage: 1,200
Bear Speed: Fast
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Bear With Me

When Nita's bear takes damage, Nita recovers 500 health. This creates a powerful symbiotic relationship between Nita and her bear companion.

Hyper Bear

Nita's bear attacks 60% faster. This dramatically increases the bear's damage output and makes it much more threatening to enemies.

Gadgets

Bear Paws

Nita's bear creates a shocking wave that stuns enemies for 1 second. Perfect for controlling enemy movement and setting up kills.

Faux Fur

Nita commands her bear to create a shield that reduces all incoming damage by 80% for 6 seconds. Makes the bear nearly invincible for a short time.
