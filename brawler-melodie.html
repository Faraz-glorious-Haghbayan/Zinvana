Melodie

The Musical Maestro

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
3,600 Health

Overview

Melodie is a versatile support brawler who uses the power of music to heal allies and debuff enemies. This harmonious character excels at providing team support through healing melodies, speed boosts, and area control. Melodie's musical abilities create zones of influence that can turn the tide of battle through strategic positioning and timing. Her unique playstyle revolves around maintaining optimal positioning to maximize the effectiveness of her musical auras while providing consistent healing and buffs to teammates. Melodie combines artistic flair with tactical prowess to become an invaluable team asset who can orchestrate victory through her musical mastery.

Abilities

Attack: Musical Notes

Main Attack

Melodie fires a stream of musical notes that deal damage to enemies and create healing zones where they land.

Damage per note: 520
Notes per shot: 4
Healing per zone: 400
Range: 7.33
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Harmony Aura

Super Ability

Melodie creates a large musical aura that heals allies, increases their movement speed, and slows down enemies within the area.

Healing per second: 600
Speed boost: 25%
Enemy slow: 35%
Duration: 6 seconds
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Resonance

Melodie's healing zones last 50% longer, and allies standing in them gain increased reload speed for 3 seconds after leaving.

Crescendo

Each consecutive musical note that hits the same enemy deals 15% more damage, stacking up to 60% increased damage.

Gadgets

Healing Chord

Melodie instantly heals herself and nearby allies for 2000 health while providing a temporary shield that absorbs 1500 damage.

Dissonance

Melodie creates a discordant sound wave that silences enemies, preventing them from using their Super abilities for 4 seconds.

Strategy & Tips

🎵

Positioning Master

Stay behind cover while maintaining optimal distance to support teammates with healing zones.

💚

Healing Priority

Focus on keeping low-health teammates alive rather than dealing maximum damage to enemies.

🎼

Aura Control

Use Harmony Aura to control key areas during team fights and objective captures.

🔄

Resonance Value

With Resonance, create healing zones in high-traffic areas for maximum team benefit.

🛡️

Emergency Heal

Save Healing Chord for critical moments when multiple teammates need instant recovery.

🔇

Silence Timing

Use Dissonance to prevent enemy Super abilities during crucial team fight moments.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for supporting the gem carrier and controlling the center with healing zones.

Brawl Ball

Great for supporting the ball carrier and creating healing zones near the goal.

Heist

Perfect for supporting teammates during pushes and defending with area control.
