Bibi

The Fierce Batter

Batter Type
Epic Rarity
5,800 Health

Overview

Bibi is a tough batter who swings her baseball bat with incredible force. This fierce melee fighter excels at close-range combat and can knock back enemies with her powerful swings. Her charged-up bat delivers devastating damage, while her Super can bounce off walls to hit enemies around corners. Bibi's aggressive playstyle and high damage potential make her perfect for players who love getting up close and personal with their enemies.

Abilities

Attack: Three Strikes

Main Attack

Bibi swings her baseball bat in a wide arc. When her bat is fully charged, it deals extra damage and knocks back enemies.

Damage: 1,260
Charged damage: 1,680
Range: 2.67
Reload: 0.8s

Super: Spitball

Super Ability

Bibi spits out a bouncy bubble that travels across the battlefield, bouncing off walls and dealing damage to enemies it hits.

Damage: 2,240
Range: Very Long
Bounces: 3 times
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Home Run

Bibi's movement speed is increased by 12% when her Home Run bar is fully charged, allowing for better positioning and engaging enemies.

Batting Stance

When Bibi's Home Run bar is fully charged, she takes 30% less damage from all sources, making her much tankier in close combat.

Gadgets

Vitamin Booster

Bibi heals 600 health per second for 4 seconds, providing sustained healing during fights or after taking damage.

Extra Sticky

Bibi's next Spitball slows down enemies by 50% for 3 seconds upon hit, making it easier to finish them off or escape.
