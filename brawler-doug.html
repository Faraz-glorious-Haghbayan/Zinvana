Doug

The Canine Companion

Support Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,480 Health

Overview

Doug is a loyal canine companion who brings unwavering loyalty and support to his team. With his keen senses and protective instincts, he excels at tracking enemies and providing valuable assistance to his teammates. Doug's faithful nature and unique tracking abilities make him an excellent support brawler who can change the flow of battle through information and utility rather than raw damage.

Abilities

Attack: Bone Throw

Main Attack

Doug throws a bone that bounces between enemies, dealing damage and marking them for 4 seconds. Marked enemies are visible through walls and bushes.

Damage: 980
Bounces: 3
Range: 7
Reload: 1.7s

Super: Pack Hunt

Super Ability

Doug howls, summoning spectral wolves that chase and attack the nearest enemies. The wolves prioritize marked enemies and deal increased damage to them.

Wolf damage: 700
Number of wolves: 4
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Keen Senses

Doug can see enemies in bushes within 6 tiles and gains 15% movement speed when moving toward marked enemies. Perfect for tracking and hunting.

Loyal Pack

Doug's Super summons an additional wolf, and all wolves heal Doug for 200 health when they hit an enemy. Great for sustain and increased damage.

Gadgets

Scent Trail

Doug creates a scent trail for 6 seconds that reveals all enemies who cross it and reduces their movement speed by 30%.

Guard Dog

Doug gains 40% damage reduction and immunity to slowing effects for 4 seconds while protecting the nearest teammate.
