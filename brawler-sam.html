Sam

The Cybernetic Assassin

Assassin Type
Mythic Rarity
5,200 Health

Overview

Sam is a cybernetic assassin enhanced with advanced technology and stealth capabilities. This futuristic warrior excels at eliminating high-priority targets through precise strikes and tactical positioning. Sam's cybernetic enhancements allow for enhanced mobility and burst damage potential, making him a formidable close-quarters combatant. His combination of stealth mechanics, technological superiority, and assassin instincts makes him a dangerous opponent who can strike from the shadows and disappear before enemies can react.

Abilities

Attack: Cyber Knuckles

Main Attack

Sam throws his cybernetic knuckles in a straight line, dealing high damage to enemies and returning to him after a short delay.

Damage: 1,400
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.9s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Cloaking Device

Super Ability

Sam activates his cloaking device, becoming invisible and gaining increased movement speed for a short duration, perfect for flanking enemies.

Duration: 4.0s
Speed boost: +30%
Next attack boost: +50% damage
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Magnetic Field

Sam's knuckles create a magnetic field that pulls enemies toward their path, making it easier to land hits and combo attacks.

Overcharge

When Sam's health drops below 40%, his attack speed increases by 25% and his knuckles deal additional damage for enhanced survivability.

Gadgets

Pulse Grenade

Sam throws a pulse grenade that explodes after a short delay, dealing area damage and temporarily disabling enemy abilities.

Phase Shift

Sam instantly teleports a short distance in the direction he's facing, allowing for quick escapes or aggressive positioning.

Strategy & Tips

👤

Stealth Approach

Use your cloaking device to get close to enemies undetected and position for maximum damage potential.

🎯

Precision Strikes

Aim your knuckles carefully to maximize damage and take advantage of the magnetic field's pull effect.

⚡

Overcharge Timing

Use Overcharge's damage boost when low on health to turn desperate situations into elimination opportunities.

💥

Grenade Control

Use Pulse Grenades to disable enemy abilities during crucial moments and create openings for your team.

🌀

Phase Positioning

Master Phase Shift for both offensive flanking and defensive escapes to maintain battlefield control.

🏃

Hit and Run

Use your mobility to strike quickly and retreat before enemies can retaliate effectively.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Perfect for picking off isolated enemies and using stealth to avoid unfavorable encounters.

Bounty

Excellent for eliminating high-value targets and escaping before enemy reinforcements arrive.

Knockout

Great for flanking enemies and securing crucial eliminations in elimination-based gameplay.
