Penny

The Pirate

Fighter Type
Epic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Penny is a crafty pirate fighter who brings explosive firepower to the battlefield with her unique coin-shooting cannon and devastating mortar turret. This treasure-hunting buccaneer excels at both long-range combat and area control, making her a versatile threat in any situation. Her ability to control key areas with her mortar while dealing consistent damage with her coin attacks makes her an excellent team player who can adapt to various combat scenarios.

Abilities

Attack: Plunderbuss

Main Attack

Penny fires a bag of coins that deals damage and splits into scattered coins that can hit multiple enemies. The split coins deal reduced damage but can hit additional targets.

Direct Damage: 1,540
Split Damage: 518 per coin
Range: 9.33
Reload: 1.9s

Super: Old Lobber

Super Ability

Penny deploys a cannon mortar that fires at the closest enemy. The mortar provides sustained area damage and can control key positions on the battlefield.

Mortar Health: 3,000
Mortar Damage: 1,680
Range: 10.67
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Last Blast

When Penny's mortar is destroyed, it explodes and deals 1,680 damage to nearby enemies. This makes destroying the mortar a risky proposition for enemies.

Balls of Fire

Penny's mortar shoots fireballs that leave burning puddles, dealing 300 damage per second for 3 seconds. This adds significant area denial capability.

Gadgets

Salty Barrel

Penny throws a barrel that explodes after 3 seconds, dealing 2,000 damage. Perfect for area control and finishing off enemies.

Captain's Compass

Penny's next attack splits into 5 coins that spread in different directions. Great for hitting multiple enemies or covering large areas.
