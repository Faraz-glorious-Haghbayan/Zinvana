Kit

The Feline Helper

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
3,800 Health

Overview

Kit is an agile support brawler who brings feline finesse to the battlefield through his cat-like abilities and team support mechanics. This nimble helper excels at providing healing, mobility boosts, and stealth assistance to teammates while maintaining impressive agility and survivability. Kit's unique gameplay revolves around positioning, timing support abilities, and using his cat instincts to outmaneuver enemies. His feline nature allows him to access tight spaces, leap over obstacles, and provide crucial support when teammates need it most. Kit combines playful charm with tactical brilliance to become an essential team player.

Abilities

Attack: Claw Swipe

Main Attack

Kit swipes with his claws in a fan pattern, dealing damage to enemies in front of him and applying a healing effect to nearby allies.

Damage: 600
Range: 4.5
Reload: 1.5s
Healing: 300

Super: Cat Leap

Super Ability

Kit leaps to a target location, becoming briefly invisible and creating a healing zone that provides continuous healing to all allies within it.

Healing per second: 800
Duration: 5 seconds
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Nine Lives

Kit gains 20% damage reduction and increased movement speed for 3 seconds after dropping below 50% health. This effect can only trigger once per life.

Catnip

Kit's healing zones also increase allies' damage output by 25% and reload speed by 30% while they remain within the healing area.

Gadgets

Yarn Ball

Kit throws a yarn ball that bounces between enemies, dealing damage and slowing them by 40% for 3 seconds.

Catnap

Kit instantly heals himself and nearby allies for 2500 health while making them all invisible for 2 seconds.
