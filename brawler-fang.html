Fang

The Kung Fu Master

Assassin Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,760 Health

Overview

Fang is a skilled martial artist who brings ancient kung fu techniques to the modern battlefield. This disciplined assassin specializes in chain combos and momentum-based attacks that can eliminate multiple enemies in succession. His unique ability to reset his Super through eliminations makes him incredibly dangerous in the right hands. Fang's mastery of martial arts philosophy combined with his competitive spirit makes him a formidable close-range fighter.

Abilities

Attack: Corn-Fu

Main Attack

Fang throws a popcorn projectile that deals damage to enemies. The projectile has a unique trajectory and moderate range.

Damage: 1,120
Range: 7.33
Reload: 1.3s
Projectile speed: Fast

Super: Roundhouse Kick

Super Ability

Fang performs a flying kick that deals massive damage to enemies. If he defeats an enemy, his Super instantly recharges for another kick.

Damage: 2,200
Range: 5.33
Reset on kill: Yes
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Fresh Kicks

Fang gains increased movement speed after using his Super, allowing him to chase down enemies or escape dangerous situations.

Divine Soles

Fang's Super charges faster, allowing him to use his devastating kicks more frequently throughout battles.

Gadgets

Corn-Fu

Fang's next attack shoots multiple popcorn projectiles in a spread pattern, increasing his damage potential.

Roundhouse Kick

Fang instantly charges his Super to maximum, allowing for immediate devastating kick attacks.

Strategy & Tips

🥋

Chain Combos

Focus on eliminating low-health enemies with your Super to chain multiple kicks and wipe enemy teams.

⚡

Speed Advantage

Use Fresh Kicks to gain movement speed after Super, allowing you to chase down fleeing enemies.

🎯

Target Priority

Always target enemies you can eliminate with your Super to trigger the reset and continue the chain.

🔄

Fast Charging

With Divine Soles, be more aggressive with your Super usage since it charges faster.

🌽

Spread Shot

Use Corn-Fu gadget to hit multiple enemies or guarantee hits on single targets at close range.

💪

Instant Power

Use Roundhouse Kick gadget for emergency eliminations or to start a chain combo immediately.

Best Game Modes

Showdown

Excellent for 1v1 eliminations and chaining Supers to eliminate multiple enemies in succession.

Knockout

Great for eliminating key enemies and using chain combos to secure team victories.

Bounty

Perfect for eliminating priority targets and using mobility to escape after successful eliminations.
