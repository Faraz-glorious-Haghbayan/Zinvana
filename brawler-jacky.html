Jacky

The Tough Demolition Worker

Heavyweight Type
Super Rare Rarity
8,500 Health

Overview

Jacky is a tough construction worker who brings her jackhammer and no-nonsense attitude to the battlefield. This heavyweight brawler excels at close-range combat with her wide-area attacks that can hit multiple enemies simultaneously. Her Super creates a powerful earthquake that pulls enemies toward her while dealing damage. Jacky's high health pool and area damage make her perfect for controlling team fights and breaking through enemy formations with brute force and determination.

Abilities

Attack: Groundbreaker

Main Attack

Jacky swings her jackhammer in a wide arc around herself, dealing damage to all enemies within range. The attack has a short range but covers a large area.

Star Powers

Counter Crush

Jacky reflects 15% of the damage she receives back to enemies who damaged her, making attackers think twice about engaging her.

Hardy Hard Hat

Jacky takes 15% less damage from all sources while her Super is charging, making her even tankier during extended fights.

Gadgets

Pneumatic Booster

Jacky's movement speed is increased by 38% for 3 seconds, allowing her to close gaps quickly or escape dangerous situations.

Rebuild

Jacky instantly rebuilds all destroyed walls within a large area around her, providing cover and altering the battlefield layout.
