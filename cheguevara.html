Che Guevara

The Revolutionary Icon and Visionary Leader

Born: June 14, 1928

Died: October 9, 1967

Nationality: Argentine/Cuban

Occupation: Revolutionary, Guerrilla Leader, Physician

Notable For: Key role in the Cuban Revolution

Early Life and Education

Ernesto "Che" Guevara was born in Rosario, Argentina. Raised in a middle-class family, Che was deeply influenced by the poverty and inequality he witnessed during his travels across Latin America. He studied medicine at the University of Buenos Aires, where his encounters with social injustice ignited a passion for revolutionary change.
