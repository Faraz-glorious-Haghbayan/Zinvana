Rosa

The Tough Botanist

Heavyweight Type
Rare Rarity
8,800 Health

Overview

Rosa is a tough botanist who combines her love of plants with her fighting skills. This heavyweight brawler excels at close-range combat with her powerful boxing glove punches and can become nearly invincible with her plant-based protective shield. Her unique ability to hide and control bushes makes her a master of ambush tactics and area control. Rosa's tankiness and burst damage potential make her perfect for players who enjoy aggressive, in-your-face combat while using nature's power to their advantage.

Abilities

Attack: Hands of Stone

Main Attack

Rosa throws a flurry of powerful punches that deal high damage at close range. Her boxing gloves pack a serious punch against nearby enemies.

Damage: 1,260
Range: 3
Reload: 1.2s
Hits: 3 punches

Super: Strong Stuff

Super Ability

Rosa creates a protective plant shield around herself that reduces all incoming damage by 70% for 3 seconds, making her nearly invincible.

Damage reduction: 70%
Duration: 3 seconds
Charges: 10 hits
Movement: Normal speed

Star Powers

Plant Life

Rosa heals 200 health per second while hiding in bushes, encouraging aggressive bush control and providing sustained healing.

Thorny Gloves

Rosa's next attack after using her Super deals 220 additional damage per hit, significantly increasing her burst damage potential.

Gadgets

Grow Light

Rosa instantly creates a large bush that provides cover and healing opportunities. Perfect for controlling key positions on the map.

Unfriendly Bushes

All bushes on the map damage enemies by 100 damage per second while they're hiding in them, denying enemy bush control.
