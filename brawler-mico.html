Mico

The Parkour Ninja

Assassin Type
Mythic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Mico is a highly mobile assassin who excels at parkour-style movement and stealth tactics. This agile brawler specializes in rapid positioning, wall-jumping, and eliminating targets with precision strikes. Mico's unique mobility makes him perfect for hit-and-run tactics and flanking maneuvers. His parkour abilities allow him to navigate complex terrain with ease, making him unpredictable and hard to pin down. Mico combines speed, agility, and stealth to become a formidable assassin who can strike from unexpected angles and vanish before enemies can retaliate.

Abilities

Attack: Ninja Stars

Main Attack

Mico throws razor-sharp ninja stars in a fan pattern that deal increased damage to enemies at close range.

Damage per star: 680
Stars per shot: 3
Range: 5.67
Reload: 1.6s

Super: Wall Jump

Super Ability

Mico can jump between walls and obstacles, gaining increased movement speed and becoming temporarily invisible between jumps.

Jump distance: 8 tiles
Invisibility: 2 seconds
Speed boost: 30%
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Shadow Step

Mico becomes invisible for 2 seconds after eliminating an enemy, allowing for quick escapes or repositioning for the next target.

Parkour Master

Mico's movement speed increases by 20% when near walls, and he can wall-jump without using his Super once every 8 seconds.

Gadgets

Smoke Bomb

Mico throws a smoke bomb that creates a concealing cloud, making him and nearby allies invisible to enemies for 4 seconds.

Grappling Hook

Mico shoots a grappling hook that pulls him instantly to any wall or obstacle within range, perfect for quick escapes.
