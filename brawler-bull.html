Bull

The Unstoppable Tank

Tank Type
Rare Rarity
7,000 Health

Overview

Bull is a massive, intimidating tank who dominates close-quarters combat with his devastating shotgun and incredible health pool. This unstoppable force charges into battle, dealing massive damage at point-blank range while soaking up enemy fire. His aggressive playstyle and ability to control tight spaces make him a nightmare for enemies who dare to get too close.

Abilities

Attack: Double-Barrel

Main Attack

Bull fires a spread of shells from his double-barrel shotgun. The damage is devastating at close range, but spreads out and weakens over distance.

Max damage: 2,800
Min damage: 1,120
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Bulldozer

Super Ability

Bull charges forward, destroying obstacles and dealing damage to any enemies in his path. Perfect for closing gaps and breaking through enemy defenses.

Damage: 2,000
Range: 11.33
Charges: 10 hits

Star Powers

Berserker

When Bull's health drops below 40%, his movement speed increases by 30%. This makes him even more dangerous when low on health, allowing for aggressive plays.

Tough Guy

When Bull's health is below 40%, he takes 30% less damage. Combined with his high health, this makes him incredibly difficult to finish off.

Gadgets

T-Bone Injector

Bull instantly heals 1,500 health. This gadget provides crucial sustain during extended fights and can turn the tide of battle.

Stomper

Bull's next attack also damages enemies behind walls within range. Perfect for hitting enemies hiding behind cover.
