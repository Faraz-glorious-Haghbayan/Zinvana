About Zinvana

Zinvana was founded with one goal in mind: to help people better understand themselves and their potential. We believe that by exploring your personality, you gain the clarity needed to make informed decisions about your relationships, career, and personal growth.

Our team consists of psychologists, data scientists, and software engineers who are passionate about crafting an accessible and engaging personality test. Every question and result has been carefully developed to offer meaningful insights, while respecting your privacy and data.

As we continue to evolve, we remain committed to transparency, user empowerment, and scientific rigor. We’re excited to have you join us on this journey of self-discovery and look forward to helping you unlock your full potential.
