Mr. P

The Hotel Manager

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Mr. P is a sophisticated penguin hotel manager who specializes in summoning penguin minions and providing area control through his briefcase attacks. This well-dressed support brawler excels at maintaining battlefield presence through his spawned penguin assistants and bouncing projectile attacks. Mr. P's business acumen translates into strategic gameplay where he can control multiple areas simultaneously through his minions while dealing consistent damage with his briefcase throws. His combination of spawner mechanics, area control, and indirect damage makes him perfect for players who enjoy strategic positioning and managing multiple units on the battlefield.

Abilities

Attack: Suitcase Sling

Main Attack

Mr. P throws his briefcase in an arc that bounces once, dealing damage to enemies it hits directly and after the bounce.

Direct damage: 980
Bounce damage: 616
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Porters

Super Ability

Mr. P summons a penguin porter base that continuously spawns penguin minions to attack enemies until the base is destroyed.

Porter health: 2,100
Porter damage: 560
Spawn rate: 3.5s
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Handle With Care

Mr. P's briefcase attack returns to him after bouncing, enabling him to hit enemies on the return path for additional damage.

Revolving Door

Mr. P's penguin porters have 50% more health and spawn penguins 50% faster, making them more durable and effective.

Gadgets

Service Bell

Mr. P instantly summons a penguin porter at his location, providing immediate battlefield presence and map control.

Snappy Sniping

Mr. P's next attack has increased range and damage, making it perfect for long-range eliminations.

Strategy & Tips

🐧

Porter Placement

Place your porter bases in strategic locations where they can spawn penguins safely and control key areas.

🎯

Bounce Shots

Use walls and terrain to bounce your briefcase attacks around corners and hit enemies behind cover.

🔄

Returning Briefcase

With Handle With Care, position yourself to catch enemies on both the throw and return path of your briefcase.

🏨

Enhanced Porters

Use Revolving Door to create more durable porter bases that provide sustained battlefield presence.

🔔

Instant Summon

Use Service Bell to immediately establish map control or create a defensive position during team fights.

🎯

Long Range

Use Snappy Sniping for crucial eliminations or to reach enemies that are normally out of range.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling lanes and providing consistent pressure through penguin spawns.

Siege

Perfect for defending the IKE turret and controlling bolts with porter bases and penguin minions.

Hot Zone

Great for controlling zones through penguin presence and bouncing briefcase attacks.
