Sandy

The Sleepy Legend

Support Type
Legendary Rarity
5,320 Health

Overview

Sandy is a sleepy but powerful legendary support brawler who controls the battlefield through his mastery of sand. Despite his constantly drowsy demeanor, Sandy possesses incredible abilities that can turn any fight in his team's favor. His sandstorm mechanics and invisibility effects make him one of the most unique and strategic brawlers in the game. Sandy's laid-back personality hides a formidable fighter who excels at providing game-changing support.

Abilities

Attack: Pebble Blast

Main Attack

Sandy hurls a handful of rocks that spread out in a cone pattern. The rocks deal good damage and have decent range, making them effective for both close and mid-range combat.

Damage per rock: 300
Number of rocks: 4
Range: 6.5
Reload: 1.9s

Super: Sandstorm

Super Ability

Sandy creates a massive sandstorm that makes all allies within it invisible to enemies. The sandstorm lasts for 9 seconds and provides excellent tactical advantages.

Duration: 9s
Area radius: 4.5 tiles
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Rude Sands

Sandy's sandstorm deals 100 damage per second to enemies inside it. This turns his defensive utility into an offensive area denial tool.

Healing Winds

Sandy's sandstorm heals all allies inside it for 250 health per second. Perfect for sustaining your team during prolonged fights.

Gadgets

Sleep Stimulator

Sandy falls asleep for 2 seconds, becoming invulnerable and healing 2,000 health. He can't move or attack but is completely safe.

Sweet Dreams

Sandy's next attack puts enemies to sleep for 1.5 seconds. Sleeping enemies can't move, attack, or use abilities but wake up if damaged.
