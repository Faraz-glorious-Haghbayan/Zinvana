Lawrie

The Robot Guardian

Tank Type
Epic Rarity
6,720 Health

Overview

Lawrie is a protective robot guardian designed to shield his teammates and control the battlefield through defensive tactics. His robust mechanical frame and advanced protective systems make him an excellent frontline defender. Lawrie excels at absorbing damage and providing his team with the space they need to operate effectively. His dutiful nature and unwavering loyalty make him the ultimate team protector.

Abilities

Attack: Guardian Pulse

Main Attack

Lawrie fires an energy pulse that deals moderate damage and has decent range. The pulse can penetrate through enemies, hitting multiple targets in a line.

Damage: 980
Range: 6
Reload: 1.8s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Shield Matrix

Super Ability

Lawrie deploys a protective shield matrix that reduces incoming damage for all nearby teammates by 40% for 6 seconds. The shield also reflects 25% of absorbed damage back to attackers.

Damage reduction: 40%
Duration: 6s
Charges: 12 hits

Star Powers

Reinforced Plating

Lawrie gains 15% damage reduction and immunity to knockback effects. When below 30% health, the damage reduction increases to 30%.

Guardian Protocol

Teammates within 4 tiles of Lawrie gain 20% damage reduction and 15% faster reload speed. Perfect for supporting the team.

Gadgets

Emergency Repair

Lawrie instantly heals 2,500 health and gains immunity to all debuffs for 4 seconds. Great for surviving burst damage.

Overcharge Mode

Lawrie's next three attacks deal 50% more damage and have 25% faster reload speed. Perfect for aggressive pushes.
