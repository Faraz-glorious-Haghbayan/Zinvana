Barley

The Bartender

Thrower Type
Rare Rarity
3,800 Health

Overview

Barley is a robotic bartender who serves up explosive cocktails to his enemies! This mechanical mixologist specializes in area denial and crowd control, throwing bottles that create damaging puddles on the ground. His ability to attack over walls and maintain sustained area damage makes him excellent for controlling key positions on the battlefield. Despite his fragile nature, Barley's strategic value and area control capabilities make him a valuable team asset.

Abilities

Attack: Undiluted

Main Attack

Barley lobs a bottle that breaks on impact, leaving a puddle that damages enemies over time. The bottle can be thrown over walls and obstacles, making it perfect for area denial.

Direct Damage: 1,260
Puddle Damage: 420 per tick
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Last Call

Super Ability

Barley hurls a flurry of five bottles in a wide arc, creating multiple puddles that deal damage over time. Perfect for area control and team fights.

Direct Damage: 1,260 per bottle
Puddle Damage: 420 per tick
Bottles: 5
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Medical Use

Barley regains 300 health per second while in his own puddles. This allows him to stay in fights longer and use his own attacks defensively.

Extra Noxious

Barley's attack and Super deal 140 extra damage over time. This significantly increases his damage output against enemies caught in puddles.

Gadgets

Sticky Syrup Mixer

Barley's next attack slows down enemies by 50% for 5 seconds. Perfect for controlling enemy movement and setting up kills.

Herbal Tonic

Barley instantly heals himself and nearby allies for 1,200 health. Great for supporting the team during crucial moments.
