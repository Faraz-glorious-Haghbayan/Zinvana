Ruffs

The Space Cadet

Support Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Ruffs is a loyal space dog who commands respect through his military training and pack leadership. This cosmic canine specializes in empowering his teammates through power-up drops and strategic support. His unique ability to enhance ally performance while maintaining solid offensive capabilities makes him a versatile support brawler. Ruffs combines loyalty, strategy, and space-age technology to lead his pack to victory.

Abilities

Attack: Double-Barrel Laser

Main Attack

Ruffs fires two parallel laser shots that deal damage to enemies. Both shots can hit the same target for maximum damage potential.

Damage per shot: 644
Max damage: 1,288
Range: 8.33
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Supply Drop

Super Ability

Ruffs calls in a supply drop that damages enemies in the impact area and provides a power-up to the first ally who picks it up.

Impact damage: 1,000
Power-up boost: +25% damage
Boost duration: Permanent
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Air Superiority

Ruffs' Super bounces and can hit enemies multiple times, dealing additional damage with each bounce for increased area control.

Field Promotion

When Ruffs defeats an enemy, all allies gain a permanent 10% damage boost that stacks throughout the match.

Gadgets

Take Cover

Ruffs creates a sand bag barrier that provides cover and blocks enemy projectiles for a limited time.

Air Support

Ruffs calls in an air strike that damages all enemies in a large area after a short delay.
