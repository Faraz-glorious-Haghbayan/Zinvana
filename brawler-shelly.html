Shelly

The Tough Fighter

Fighter Type
Trophy Road Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Shelly is a tough, no-nonsense fighter who isn't afraid to get up close and personal with her enemies. Armed with a powerful shotgun, she excels in close-quarters combat and can quickly eliminate enemies who dare to come too close. As the starter brawler, she's reliable, straightforward, and perfect for players learning the basics of Brawl Stars.

Abilities

Attack: Buckshot

Main Attack

Shelly fires a blast of shotgun pellets that spread out over distance. The damage is devastating at close range but decreases as enemies get further away.

Max damage: 2,240
Min damage: 896
Range: 7.33
Reload: 1.5s

Super: Super Shell

Super Ability

Shelly fires a devastating shotgun blast that can destroy cover and deal massive damage. It also knocks back enemies, creating space for positioning.

Max damage: 3,360
Min damage: 1,344
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Shell Shock

Shelly's Super slows down enemies for 3 seconds. This makes it easier to finish off enemies and prevents them from escaping after taking massive damage.

Band-Aid

When Shelly's health drops below 40%, she instantly heals 1,800 health. This ability has a 20-second cooldown and provides crucial survivability.

Gadgets

Fast Forward

Shelly dashes forward, dealing 1,000 damage to enemies in her path and gaining a temporary speed boost. Perfect for closing gaps or escaping danger.

Clay Pigeons

Shelly's next main attack fires in a straight line without spreading. This increases the effective range and makes it easier to hit distant targets.
