About James Charles

James Charles Dickinson, known professionally as James Charles, is an American beauty YouTuber and makeup artist who revolutionized the beauty industry by becoming the first male spokesperson for CoverGirl in 2016. At just 17 years old, he broke traditional gender barriers in the beauty space and inspired millions of young people to express themselves through makeup artistry.

Born on May 23, 1999, in Bethlehem, New York, James discovered his passion for makeup during high school when he began experimenting with different looks and techniques. His artistic talent, combined with his bold personality and willingness to challenge beauty norms, quickly gained attention on social media platforms.

James Charles is known for his incredible technical skill in makeup application, his educational content that teaches complex techniques, and his collaborations with major celebrities and brands. Despite facing various controversies throughout his career, he has maintained his position as one of the most influential beauty creators on the platform, consistently producing high-quality content that showcases both artistry and entertainment value.
