Amber

The Fire-Wielding Entertainer

Fighter Type
Legendary Rarity
4,600 Health

Overview

Amber is a fire-wielding entertainer who specializes in continuous damage and area control with her flame-based attacks. This legendary fighter excels at sustained damage output with her unique ammo system that allows for continuous fire streams. Her Super creates flaming puddles that damage enemies over time and can be ignited for additional area damage. Amber's playstyle focuses on maintaining pressure and controlling areas with persistent fire damage, making her excellent for zone control and team fights.

Abilities

Attack: Torch Song

Main Attack

Amber shoots a continuous stream of fire that drains her ammo while held down. The flame deals damage over time and has a wide spread.

Damage per hit: 320
Fire rate: Very fast
Range: 6.67
Ammo: Continuous

Super: Dragonbreath

Super Ability

Amber throws a bottle of flammable liquid that creates a fire puddle. The puddle can be ignited by her attacks for additional damage and area control.

Puddle damage: 300/sec
Ignited damage: 2,000
Duration: 10 seconds
Charges: 12 hits

Star Powers

Wild Flames

Amber's attack spreads to nearby enemies when hitting a target, allowing her to damage multiple enemies with sustained fire streams.

Scorchin' Siphon

Amber heals 800 health for each enemy defeated by her or her fire puddles, providing excellent sustain during extended fights.

Gadgets

Fire Starters

Amber drops a fire puddle at her current location that lasts for 5 seconds. Perfect for area denial and escape routes.

Dancing Flames

Amber's next attack pierces through enemies and obstacles, allowing her to hit enemies behind cover and deal with multiple targets.
