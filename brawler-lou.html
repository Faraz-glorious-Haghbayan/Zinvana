Lou

The Ice Cream Vendor

Support Type
Chromatic Rarity
3,920 Health

Overview

Lou is a cheerful ice cream vendor who brings a literal chill to the battlefield. His unique freezing mechanics allow him to slow down and eventually freeze enemies completely, making him an exceptional crowd control support brawler. Lou's ability to stack "brain freeze" effects on enemies creates tactical opportunities for his team while providing area denial through his icy super. His friendly demeanor contrasts with his ability to completely shut down enemy movements.

Abilities

Attack: Frost Bite

Main Attack

Lou shoots ice cream that pierces through enemies. Each hit applies a "Hypothermia" stack, slowing enemies and eventually freezing them solid.

Damage: 840
Freeze stacks: 5 to freeze
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.6s

Super: Can-Avalanche

Super Ability

Lou creates a large ice block that bounces around the battlefield, dealing damage and applying Hypothermia stacks to all enemies it hits.

Damage per hit: 800
Bounces: 4
Hypothermia stacks: 1 per hit
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Supercool

Lou's Super creates an icy area that grants him immunity to slowing and freezing effects, plus increased movement speed.

Hypothermia

Enemies need only 4 Hypothermia stacks to freeze instead of 5, making Lou's crowd control more efficient.

Gadgets

Ice Block

Lou creates an ice wall that blocks movement and projectiles. The wall can be destroyed by dealing enough damage to it.

Cryo Syrup

Lou's next attack slows enemies significantly for several seconds, even without Hypothermia stacks.

Strategy & Tips

🧊

Stack Management

Focus on building Hypothermia stacks on priority targets. Frozen enemies are sitting ducks for your team.

🎯

Piercing Power

Use piercing attacks to hit multiple enemies and build stacks on several targets simultaneously.

🏐

Bouncing Ball

Aim your Super at walls to control the direction and maximize hits on clustered enemies.

🛡️

Ice Wall Defense

Use Ice Block gadget to block enemy escape routes or protect teammates from incoming damage.

⚡

Faster Freeze

With Hypothermia star power, you can freeze enemies quicker, making crowd control more efficient.

🏃

Mobile Control

With Supercool, become immune to enemy slows and move faster in your Super's area.

Best Game Modes

Brawl Ball

Excellent for freezing ball carriers and controlling key areas around the goal for defensive plays.

Gem Grab

Great for protecting the gem carrier and freezing enemies who try to push forward aggressively.

Hot Zone

Perfect for controlling zones by freezing enemies and denying access to contested areas.
