About MrBeast

Jimmy Donaldson, known worldwide as MrBeast, is an American YouTuber, entrepreneur, and philanthropist who has fundamentally changed the landscape of content creation. Born on May 7, 1998, in Kansas and raised in North Carolina, Jimmy transformed from a teenager making gaming videos to becoming one of the most influential and highest-earning content creators in the world.

Starting his YouTube journey in 2012 at age 13, MrBeast initially focused on gaming content and commentary videos. However, his breakthrough came when he shifted to creating elaborate challenge videos, massive giveaways, and philanthropic content that often costs hundreds of thousands or even millions of dollars to produce.

What sets MrBeast apart is his unique combination of entertainment and philanthropy. His videos regularly feature life-changing prizes, charitable donations, and community impact projects. From planting 20 million trees to giving away private islands and millions of dollars, he has redefined what's possible in content creation while inspiring millions to be more generous and community-minded.
