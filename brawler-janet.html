Janet

The Stunt Performer

Damage Dealer Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Janet is a fearless stunt performer who combines spectacular aerial maneuvers with explosive damage output. This daredevil brawler excels at mobility and positioning through her unique flight mechanics. Her rocket-powered abilities allow her to soar over obstacles, escape danger, and rain down destruction from above. Janet's theatrical flair and acrobatic skills make her a dynamic damage dealer who can strike from unexpected angles and control the battlefield through superior positioning.

Abilities

Attack: High Explosives

Main Attack

Janet fires a rocket that deals area damage on impact, with the explosion radius increasing based on distance traveled.

Damage: 1,200
Range: 8.67
Reload: 2.1s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Drop the Bass

Super Ability

Janet launches herself into the air, becoming invulnerable and able to drop dynamite on enemies below before landing.

Dynamite damage: 1,400
Flight duration: 3.5s
Range: Global
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Stage View

Janet's attack range increases by 20% and she can see enemies in bushes when airborne, providing superior battlefield awareness.

Vocal Warm-Up

Janet charges her Super 30% faster, allowing for more frequent aerial assaults and positioning advantages.

Gadgets

Drop the Bass

Janet's next attack creates a larger explosion and knocks back enemies, providing crowd control and area denial.

Backup Vocals

When Janet uses her Super, she spawns a clone that mimics her attacks for a short duration, doubling her damage output.

Strategy & Tips

🚀

Rocket Mastery

Aim for maximum range to increase explosion radius and damage potential with your rocket attacks.

✈️

Flight Timing

Use your Super to escape danger, reposition for better angles, or avoid enemy ultimates while dealing damage.

💣

Dynamite Drops

Drop dynamite on grouped enemies, objectives, or chokepoints for maximum area control and damage.

👁️

Bush Vision

With Stage View, use your flight to reveal bush campers and provide intel for your team.

⚡

Super Charging

Vocal Warm-Up allows more frequent flights - use this for consistent pressure and positioning.

👥

Clone Tactics

Coordinate Backup Vocals with team pushes to overwhelm enemies with doubled damage output.

Best Game Modes

Bounty

Excellent for long-range sniping and escaping danger while maintaining vision control over the battlefield.

Siege

Perfect for bombarding the IKE turret from above and controlling bolt spawn areas with area damage.

Knockout

Great for pick-offs and repositioning, with the ability to escape unfavorable engagements through flight.
