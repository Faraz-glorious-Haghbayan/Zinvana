Historical Timeline

Key moments that shaped human civilization

550 BCE
Cyrus the Great founds the Persian Empire

The first great multicultural empire with revolutionary policies of tolerance and human rights.

27 BCE
Augustus becomes first Roman Emperor

The Roman Republic transforms into an empire that would dominate the Mediterranean for centuries.

1206 CE
Genghis Khan unites the Mongol tribes

The beginning of the largest contiguous land empire in human history.

1453 CE
Ottoman Empire conquers Constantinople

The fall of the Byzantine Empire and the rise of Ottoman dominance in Europe and Asia.

1789 CE
French Revolution begins

The birth of modern democracy and the end of absolute monarchy in Europe.
