Gene

The Mystical Genie

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Gene is a mystical support brawler who combines magical attacks with powerful crowd control abilities. This ancient genie excels at manipulating enemy positions and providing crucial support to his team through his unique pull mechanic. Gene's magical powers allow him to change the course of battles by repositioning enemies and creating opportunities for his teammates. His combination of decent damage output, crowd control, and healing abilities makes him one of the most versatile support brawlers in the game. Gene is perfect for players who enjoy strategic gameplay and making game-changing plays.

Abilities

Attack: Magic Puffs

Main Attack

Gene shoots a spread of magical projectiles that deal damage to enemies. The attack has a wide spread pattern making it effective at medium range.

Damage per projectile: 364
Total damage: 1,092
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Magic Hand

Super Ability

Gene extends a magical hand that grabs the first enemy it hits and pulls them toward him, dealing damage and potentially disrupting enemy formations.

Damage: 1,000
Range: 9.33
Pull distance: 7.0
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Magic Puffs

Gene's attacks heal nearby allies for 400 health per projectile that hits an enemy, providing consistent team support during battles.

Spirit Slap

Gene's Super deals 300 additional damage and slows the pulled enemy by 50% for 2 seconds, making follow-up attacks easier to land.

Gadgets

Lamp Blowout

Gene instantly teleports back to his previous position from 3 seconds ago, providing an escape mechanism or repositioning tool.

Vengeful Spirits

Gene creates a spirit that follows and attacks the nearest enemy, dealing damage over time and providing additional map control.
