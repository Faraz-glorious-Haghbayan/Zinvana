Rico

The Bouncing Bullet

Sharpshooter Type
Super Rare Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Rico is a slick robot sharpshooter who specializes in ricochet shots and trick angles. This mechanical marksman can bounce his bullets off walls to hit enemies hiding behind cover, making him deadly in maps with lots of obstacles. His unique shooting mechanics reward players who can master geometry and predict bullet trajectories. Rico's ability to hit enemies from unexpected angles makes him a constant threat on the battlefield.

Abilities

Attack: Bouncy Bullets

Main Attack

Rico fires a burst of bullets that can bounce off walls. The bullets gain +26% damage after bouncing, making ricochet shots more powerful than direct hits.

Direct Damage: 448 per bullet
Bounce Damage: 564 per bullet
Range: 10.67
Reload: 1.7s

Super: Trick Shot

Super Ability

Rico fires a long burst of bullets that bounce off walls. The bullets spread out in a cone pattern, making it easier to hit multiple enemies or cover large areas.

Direct Damage: 320 per bullet
Bounce Damage: 404 per bullet
Bullets: 16
Charges: 12 hits

Star Powers

Super Bouncy

Rico's bullets can now bounce an additional time and travel 26% farther. This dramatically increases his range and trick shot potential.

Robo Retreat

When Rico drops below 40% health, his movement speed increases by 34%. This helps him escape dangerous situations and reposition.

Gadgets

Multiball Launcher

Rico's next attack shoots twice as many bullets. Perfect for dealing massive burst damage or covering larger areas.

Bouncy Castle

Rico bounces over the nearest wall and gains a shield that reduces damage by 30% for 3 seconds. Great for escaping or repositioning.
