Bea

The Skilled Beekeeper

Sharpshooter Type
Epic Rarity
3,200 Health

Overview

Bea is a skilled beekeeper who uses her knowledge of bees to control the battlefield with precision and power. This sharpshooter excels at long-range combat with a unique charged shot mechanic that rewards accurate aim. When Bea hits an enemy with her regular shot, her next attack becomes a powerful charged shot that deals massive damage. Her Super releases a swarm of bees that slow and damage enemies over time. Bea's playstyle rewards patience, positioning, and precise aim.

Abilities

Attack: Big Sting

Main Attack

Bea fires a long-range shot. When she hits an enemy, her next shot becomes supercharged, dealing significantly more damage.

Normal damage: 1,120
Charged damage: 2,240
Range: 11
Reload: 1.4s

Super: Bee Swarm

Super Ability

Bea throws a bee hive that releases a swarm of bees in a large area, slowing enemies and dealing damage over time.

Damage per second: 1,000
Slow effect: 50%
Duration: 6 seconds
Charges: 5 hits

Star Powers

Insta Beeload

When Bea defeats an enemy with her charged shot, she instantly gets another charged shot ready, allowing for potential double eliminations.

Honeyed Coating

When Bea takes damage, she gains a shield that reduces the next hit by 20% and charges her next shot, providing defensive utility.

Gadgets

Honey Molasses

Bea instantly slows down all enemies within a large area around her by 75% for 4 seconds. Perfect for escaping or setting up eliminations.

Rattled Hive

Bea's next shot bounces between enemies up to 3 times, potentially hitting multiple targets with her charged shot damage.
