Byron

The Snake Oil Salesman

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Byron is a mysterious snake oil salesman who masters the art of dual-purpose medicine. His unique ability to both heal allies and poison enemies with the same attack makes him one of the most versatile support brawlers in the game. Byron's pharmaceutical expertise allows him to turn the tide of battle through strategic positioning and timing. His poison mechanics reward patience and precision, making him a high-skill support brawler.

Abilities

Attack: Snake Oil

Main Attack

Byron shoots a long-range projectile that heals allies over time or deals poison damage to enemies over time. The effect stacks up to 3 times.

Heal per tick: 364
Poison per tick: 364
Duration: 4s
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Miracle Tonic

Super Ability

Byron throws a bottle that creates a puddle on the ground. The puddle heals allies and damages enemies over time while they stand in it.

Heal per second: 1,200
Damage per second: 1,200
Duration: 5s
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Malaise

Enemies take 25% more damage from all sources while they're poisoned by Byron's attacks.

Injection

When Byron's allies are healed by his attacks, they also gain a temporary 25% damage boost for a short duration.

Gadgets

Shot in the Arm

Byron's next attack pierces through enemies and continues to heal allies or damage enemies behind them.

Booster Shots

Byron instantly reloads all his ammo, allowing for immediate follow-up shots and sustained support or aggression.
