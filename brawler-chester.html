Chester

The Jester

Damage Dealer Type
Legendary Rarity
4,800 Health

Overview

Chester is an unpredictable jester who brings chaos to the battlefield through his random ability mechanics. This mischievous brawler excels at keeping enemies guessing with his constantly changing arsenal of tricks and surprises. Chester's unique gameplay revolves around adaptability and making the most of whatever random ability he receives. His unpredictable nature makes him both challenging to master and exciting to play, as no two matches are exactly the same. Chester combines humor, chaos, and strategic unpredictability to create memorable battlefield moments.

Abilities

Attack: Jester's Bells

Main Attack

Chester throws jingling bells in a spread pattern that deal damage to enemies and have a chance to apply random effects on hit.

Damage per bell: 560
Bells per shot: 3
Range: 6.33
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Wild Card

Super Ability

Chester's Super randomly selects from a pool of powerful abilities, ranging from healing to damage buffs to area control effects.

Possible effects: 8+ different
Effect duration: Varies
Randomness: 100%
Charges: 10 hits

Star Powers

Bell O' Mania

Chester's bells have a 25% chance to apply a random debuff to enemies, including slow, stun, or poison effects for chaos.

Spicy Dice

Chester gains a random buff at the start of each match that lasts the entire game, providing unpredictable advantages.

Gadgets

Candy Beans

Chester throws candy that heals allies and damages enemies, with the effect being randomly determined for each target.

Prankster

Chester swaps positions with a random enemy on the battlefield, creating chaos and potential strategic advantages.
