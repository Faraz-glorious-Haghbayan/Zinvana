Eve

The Hive Mind

Damage Dealer Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,000 Health

Overview

Eve is a mysterious hive mind entity that commands swarms of hatchlings to overwhelm her enemies. This alien brawler specializes in area control through spawning and commanding multiple units simultaneously. Her unique ability to create autonomous minions while maintaining ranged damage output makes her a formidable area control brawler. Eve combines strategic spawning, swarm tactics, and alien technology to dominate the battlefield through sheer numbers.

Abilities

Attack: Spawn Egg

Main Attack

Eve fires an egg projectile that travels over walls and spawns a hatchling when it lands, which will seek out and attack nearby enemies.

Egg damage: 644
Hatchling damage: 700
Range: 8.67
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Big Baby

Super Ability

Eve spawns a large hatchling that has significantly more health and damage than regular hatchlings, creating a powerful minion ally.

Big Baby health: 4,000
Big Baby damage: 1,400
Duration: 12s
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Unnatural Order

Eve's hatchlings deal 25% more damage to enemies with lower health than them, making them excellent finishers.

Happy Surprise

When a hatchling is destroyed, it explodes dealing area damage to nearby enemies for additional battlefield control.

Gadgets

Gotta Go!

Eve's next attack spawns a hatchling that moves 50% faster, allowing for quick strikes and repositioning.

Motherly Love

Eve instantly heals all active hatchlings and Big Baby, extending their battlefield presence and effectiveness.

Strategy & Tips

🥚

Egg Placement

Launch eggs over walls to spawn hatchlings behind enemy cover and control key battlefield positions.

👶

Big Baby Timing

Deploy Big Baby when enemies are grouped or to push objectives for maximum impact and area control.

⚡

Speed Advantage

Use Gotta Go! for quick flanking maneuvers or to escape dangerous situations with faster hatchlings.

💚

Healing Support

Time Motherly Love to extend your minions' lifespan during crucial team fights and objective pushes.

💥

Explosion Tactics

With Happy Surprise, position hatchlings strategically so their death explosions damage grouped enemies.

🎯

Low Health Targets

Target wounded enemies with Unnatural Order to maximize hatchling damage and secure eliminations.

Best Game Modes

Siege

Perfect for overwhelming the IKE turret with swarms of hatchlings and controlling the bolt spawn area.

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling the gem mine area and creating pressure with multiple units simultaneously.

Hot Zone

Great for occupying multiple zones with hatchlings while maintaining safe distance with ranged attacks.
