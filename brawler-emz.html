Emz

The Trendy Undead Influencer

Thrower Type
Epic Rarity
5,320 Health

Overview

Emz is a trendy undead influencer who controls the battlefield with her toxic spray attacks. This fashionable zombie excels at area control and crowd management, using her hairspray to create damaging clouds that slow and damage enemies over time. Her Super creates a massive toxic cloud that pushes enemies away while dealing continuous damage. Emz's unique playstyle focuses on controlling space and keeping enemies at the perfect distance, making her ideal for players who enjoy strategic positioning and area denial.

Abilities

Attack: Toxic Clouds

Main Attack

Emz sprays a cloud of hairspray that deals more damage to enemies the further they are from her. The spray lingers and damages enemies over time.

Min damage: 504
Max damage: 756
Range: 7.67
Reload: 2.2s

Super: Caustic Charisma

Super Ability

Emz creates a massive toxic cloud around herself that pushes enemies away and deals damage over time to any enemies caught within it.

Damage per tick: 300
Duration: 4 seconds
Range: 4.67
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Bad Karma

Enemies that stay within Emz's Super area for more than 2 seconds take 50% more damage from all sources, making them much more vulnerable.

Hype

Emz heals 420 health for each enemy brawler hit by her attack, providing sustain during team fights and encouraging aggressive positioning.

Gadgets

Friendzoner

Emz pushes back all enemies within a close range and slows them down for 2 seconds, perfect for creating space when enemies get too close.

Acid Spray

Emz's next attack deals 25% more damage and destroys bushes and obstacles, revealing hidden enemies and breaking cover.

Strategy & Tips

📏

Distance Control

Keep enemies at medium range where your attack deals maximum damage. Too close or too far reduces your effectiveness.

☁️

Area Denial

Use your Super to control choke points and deny enemy access to important areas like gems or zones.

💔

Bad Karma Setup

Force enemies to stay in your Super cloud, then follow up with attacks for massive damage with the vulnerability effect.

💚

Healing Strategy

With Hype, target multiple enemies with your spray to maximize healing and sustain in team fights.

🛡️

Close Range Defense

Use Friendzoner to push back aggressive melee brawlers and create the distance you need to be effective.

🌿

Bush Control

Acid Spray can clear bushes and reveal hidden enemies, preventing ambushes and controlling vision.

Best Game Modes

Hot Zone

Perfect for controlling zones with her area denial. Can keep enemies out of contested areas with toxic clouds.

Gem Grab

Excellent at controlling the mine area and protecting gem carriers. Can deny enemy access to crucial positions.

Siege

Great for controlling the bolt spawns and defending against enemy siege attacks with area denial.
