Lola

The Drama Queen

Damage Dealer Type
Chromatic Rarity
3,920 Health

Overview

Lola is a dramatic movie star who brings Hollywood glamour and ego-driven combat to the battlefield. This theatrical damage dealer specializes in creating her own spotlight moments through her unique Ego mechanic that spawns a mirror image of herself. Her ability to literally double her presence on the battlefield makes her a formidable opponent who can control multiple angles simultaneously. Lola's narcissistic personality translates into devastating self-improvement abilities.

Abilities

Attack: Freeze Frame

Main Attack

Lola shoots a single projectile that deals damage to enemies. Simple but effective for consistent damage output at medium range.

Damage: 1,120
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.6s
Projectile speed: Fast

Super: Ego

Super Ability

Lola spawns her Ego, a mirror image that mimics her attacks and movements. The Ego has reduced health but identical damage output.

Ego health: 1,960
Ego damage: 1,120
Duration: 10s
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Improvise

When Lola's Ego is destroyed, she gains increased damage and movement speed for a short duration, turning loss into advantage.

Sealed with a Kiss

Lola's Ego explodes when destroyed, dealing damage to nearby enemies and providing one final dramatic moment.

Gadgets

Freeze Frame

Lola's Ego becomes invulnerable for a short duration, allowing it to survive in dangerous situations and continue fighting.

Stunt Double

Lola instantly teleports to her Ego's location, perfect for repositioning or escaping dangerous situations.

Strategy & Tips

🎭

Ego Positioning

Position your Ego to attack from different angles and create crossfire situations that enemies can't easily escape.

🎬

Double Damage

Coordinate attacks with your Ego to deal massive combined damage and eliminate enemies quickly.

⚡

Improvise Power

With Improvise, don't be afraid to let your Ego take risks - the damage boost when it's destroyed can be game-changing.

💥

Explosive Finale

Use Sealed with a Kiss to position your Ego near enemies before it's destroyed for maximum explosion damage.

🛡️

Freeze Protection

Use Freeze Frame gadget to protect your Ego during crucial moments or when it's under heavy fire.

🔄

Teleport Tactics

Use Stunt Double to quickly reposition, escape danger, or surprise enemies with unexpected flanking maneuvers.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling multiple lanes and providing double the pressure on enemy gem carriers.

Knockout

Great for eliminating enemies with coordinated attacks and using Ego for flanking maneuvers.

Showdown

Perfect for 1v1 situations where her Ego effectively makes it a 2v1 encounter against other brawlers.
