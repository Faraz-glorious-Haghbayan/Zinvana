Mahatma Gandhi

The Peaceful Leader and Champion of Nonviolence

Born: October 2, 1869

Died: January 30, 1948

Nationality: Indian

Occupation: Lawyer, Politician, Activist

Notable For: Leading the Indian Independence Movement through Nonviolence

Early Life and Education

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, popularly known as Mahatma Gandhi, was born in Porbandar, India. Raised in a modest family, Gandhi was deeply influenced by the values of truth and nonviolence from an early age. His education in law in London and his experiences in South Africa further shaped his belief in justice and equality.
