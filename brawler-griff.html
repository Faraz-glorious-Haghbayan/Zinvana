Griff

The Shop Owner

Fighter Type
Chromatic Rarity
5,600 Health

Overview

Griff is a money-obsessed shop owner who brings his business acumen and cash-throwing skills to the battlefield. This entrepreneurial fighter specializes in close to mid-range combat using his signature cashier's register as a weapon. His unique attack pattern and business-minded approach to battle make him a formidable opponent who literally throws money at his problems. Griff's profit-driven mentality translates into efficient and aggressive combat tactics.

Abilities

Attack: Cashback

Main Attack

Griff shoots 9 banknotes in a wide spread pattern. The closer the enemy, the more banknotes hit for maximum damage.

Damage per note: 196
Max damage: 1,764
Range: 6
Reload: 2.1s

Super: Coin Shower

Super Ability

Griff throws a bag of coins that explodes on impact, dealing damage in a large area and leaving coins that heal allies.

Explosion damage: 1,200
Coin heal: 800
Coin duration: 10s
Charges: 12 hits

Star Powers

Business Resilience

Griff gains increased damage resistance when his health is low, making him harder to eliminate in critical moments.

Keep the Change

Griff's Super spawns additional coins that provide more healing opportunities for allies in the area.

Gadgets

Piggy Bank

Griff drops a piggy bank that explodes after a delay, dealing area damage to nearby enemies.

Wallet Magnet

Griff pulls all nearby enemies toward him while dealing damage, perfect for grouping enemies for elimination.
