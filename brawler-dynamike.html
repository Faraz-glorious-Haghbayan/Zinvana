Dynamike

The Explosive Expert

Thrower Type
Super Rare Rarity
4,400 Health

Overview

Dynamike is an old miner turned explosive expert who loves to blow things up! With his trusty bag of dynamite, he can lob explosives over walls and obstacles to reach enemies hiding behind cover. His explosive attacks deal massive area damage, making him deadly against grouped enemies. Though he moves slowly and has relatively low health, his unique throwing mechanics and devastating area damage make him a formidable opponent when positioned correctly.

Abilities

Attack: Short Fuse

Main Attack

Dynamike tosses two sticks of dynamite that explode after a short delay. The dynamite can be thrown over walls and obstacles, dealing area damage to enemies caught in the blast radius.

Damage: 1,260 per stick
Range: 8.67
Reload: 2.1s
Explosion Radius: 2.67

Super: Big Barrel o' Boom

Super Ability

Dynamike hurls a massive barrel that explodes on impact, dealing tremendous area damage. The explosion also destroys walls and obstacles, opening up new tactical opportunities.

Damage: 2,800
Range: 9.33
Explosion Radius: 4.67
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Dyna-Jump

Dynamike can jump with his Super's explosion if he's close enough to the blast. This provides incredible mobility and escape options for the old miner.

Demolition

Dynamike's main attack deals +1,000 damage to enemies below 50% health. This makes him extremely dangerous to weakened opponents.

Gadgets

Fidget Spinner

Dynamike spins around and throws five sticks of dynamite in different directions. Perfect for area denial and hitting multiple enemies.

Satchel Charge

Dynamike drops a big bomb behind him that explodes after 3 seconds, dealing massive damage. Great for area control and self-defense.

Strategy & Tips

💣

Wall Advantage

Use your ability to throw over walls to attack enemies who think they're safe behind cover. This is your biggest tactical advantage.

🎯

Predict Movement

Your dynamite has a delay before exploding. Learn to predict where enemies will be when the explosion occurs for maximum damage.

🌊

Area Control

Use your explosives to control key areas and force enemies to move where you want them. Zone enemies away from objectives.

🏃

Dyna-Jump Mastery

With Dyna-Jump, use your Super near yourself to escape dangerous situations or reach unexpected positions on the map.

🛡️

Stay Behind Cover

Dynamike is fragile and slow. Always stay behind walls and use your range advantage to avoid direct confrontations.

💥

Demolition Timing

With Demolition Star Power, focus on finishing off weakened enemies for massive bonus damage. Coordinate with teammates.

Best Game Modes

Heist

Excellent for defending the safe with area damage and wall destruction. Can control choke points effectively.

Bounty

Great for controlling center areas and picking off enemies from behind cover. Wall-throwing advantage is crucial.

Siege

Perfect for defending and attacking with area damage. Can destroy walls to create new paths for the robot.
