Berry

The Sweet Supporter

Support Type
Super Rare Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Berry is a sweet and caring support brawler who specializes in healing and boosting her teammates. With her fruit-based abilities and cheerful personality, she brings both utility and positivity to any team composition. Berry excels at keeping her team healthy and providing various buffs that can turn the tide of battle. Her nurturing nature and powerful support abilities make her invaluable in team-based modes.

Abilities

Attack: Berry Burst

Main Attack

Berry throws healing berries that deal moderate damage to enemies but heal teammates they hit. The berries bounce once, potentially hitting multiple targets.

Damage: 840
Healing: 1,200
Range: 6.5
Reload: 1.5s

Super: Fruit Festival

Super Ability

Berry creates a large healing aura that continuously heals all teammates within its range for 8 seconds. The aura also provides a speed boost to allies inside it.

Healing per second: 400
Speed boost: +25%
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Sweet Essence

Berry's healing abilities also provide a 15% damage boost to healed teammates for 4 seconds. Perfect for enabling aggressive plays.

Berry Boost

Teammates healed by Berry gain 20% faster reload speed for 5 seconds. Great for sustaining team pressure and ability usage.

Gadgets

Vitamin Rush

Berry instantly heals all nearby teammates for 1,500 health and removes all debuffs from them. Perfect for emergency situations.

Super Berry

Berry's next attack creates a healing zone where it lands, continuously healing teammates in the area for 4 seconds.
