Meeple

The Board Game Master

Support Type
Legendary Rarity
3,200 Health

Overview

Meeple is a legendary brawler themed around board games who brings strategic depth to every match. This unique support character can place tiles and manipulate the battlefield, creating advantages for his team and obstacles for enemies. His dice-based attacks and tile mechanics make him one of the most tactically complex brawlers in the game, rewarding players who think several moves ahead.

Abilities

Attack: Dice Throw

Main Attack

Meeple throws enchanted dice that bounce and deal damage to enemies. The dice can hit multiple targets as they ricochet across the battlefield, making positioning crucial for maximum damage output.

Damage per die: 720
Dice count: 2
Range: 7.33
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Game Board

Super Ability

Meeple places special game tiles on the battlefield that provide various effects to allies standing on them. These tiles can boost speed, provide shields, or heal teammates, making strategic tile placement essential for victory.

Tile duration: 8 seconds
Effect radius: 3 tiles
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

House Rules

Meeple's game tiles last 3 seconds longer and provide 20% stronger effects. This extends your battlefield control and makes your support even more impactful.

Critical Roll

Meeple's dice attacks have a 25% chance to deal double damage, represented by rolling a "critical hit." High risk, high reward gameplay for aggressive players.

Gadgets

Shuffle Board

Meeple instantly rearranges all active game tiles to new random positions within range. Perfect for adapting to changing battle conditions or surprising enemies.

Bonus Turn

Meeple gains an extra attack that doesn't consume ammo. Use this for burst damage or to quickly charge your Super in crucial moments.
