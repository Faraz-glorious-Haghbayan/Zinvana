Clancy

The Tech Controller

Controller Type
Mythic Rarity
4,600 Health

Overview

Clancy is a high-tech controller brawler who dominates the battlefield through advanced robotics and strategic area control. This mechanical mastermind excels at deploying technological traps, controlling enemy movement, and providing tactical support through his array of gadgets and systems. Clancy's gameplay revolves around map control, strategic positioning, and using his technological arsenal to dictate the flow of battle. His robotic nature allows him to process battlefield information quickly and respond with precise mechanical execution. Clancy combines futuristic technology with tactical brilliance to become a formidable controller who can shut down enemy advances and create opportunities for his team.

Abilities

Attack: Plasma Bolts

Main Attack

Clancy fires plasma bolts that pierce through enemies and leave electric zones that slow and damage enemies who walk through them.

Damage: 680
Range: 7.5
Reload: 1.8s
Zone damage: 250/sec

Super: Tech Turret

Super Ability

Clancy deploys a high-tech turret that automatically targets and shoots at enemies within its range while providing a shield for allies.

Turret damage: 400
Turret health: 2,800
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

System Overload

Clancy's electric zones chain to nearby enemies, spreading the slow effect and dealing additional damage to all connected targets.

Auto Repair

Clancy's tech turret automatically repairs itself by 200 health per second and gains increased firing rate when below 50% health.

Gadgets

EMP Blast

Clancy releases an electromagnetic pulse that disables all enemy gadgets and slows their movement by 50% for 4 seconds.

Force Field

Clancy creates a protective barrier that absorbs 3000 damage and reflects 25% of absorbed damage back to attackers.

Strategy & Tips

🤖

Zone Control

Use plasma bolts to create electric zones in key areas, forcing enemies to take alternate routes.

🔧

Turret Placement

Position tech turrets in strategic locations to maximize area coverage and provide team support.

⚡

Chain Reaction

With System Overload, target grouped enemies to spread electric effects across multiple targets.

🔋

Turret Synergy

Use Auto Repair to maintain turret presence in key areas and increase pressure on enemies.

💥

Disable Tactics

Use EMP Blast to disable enemy gadgets during crucial team fights or objective moments.

🛡️

Defensive Barrier

Activate Force Field when under heavy fire to absorb damage and punish attackers.

Best Game Modes

Heist

Excellent for defending the safe with turrets and controlling enemy approach routes with electric zones.

Hot Zone

Perfect for maintaining zone control with persistent electric effects and turret coverage.

Siege

Great for controlling the bolt area and providing defensive support with tech turrets.
