Colt

The Confident Gunslinger

Sharpshooter Type
Trophy Road Rarity
2,800 Health

Overview

Colt is a confident and charismatic gunslinger who believes he's the star of the show. Armed with dual pistols, he unleashes rapid-fire attacks that can devastate enemies caught in the open. His aggressive playstyle and high damage output make him perfect for players who like to take control of the battlefield and eliminate threats quickly.

Abilities

Attack: Six-Shooters

Main Attack

Colt fires six bullets in a straight line with long range. Each bullet deals moderate damage, but the combined damage can be devastating if all bullets hit.

Damage per bullet: 448
Total damage: 2,688
Range: 10.67
Reload: 1.6s

Super: Bullet Storm

Super Ability

Colt fires a massive barrage of bullets that destroys cover and deals massive damage. The attack has a very long range and can pierce through enemies.

Damage per bullet: 320
Bullets: 12
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Slick Boots

Colt's movement speed is increased by 10%. This allows him to reposition more effectively and dodge enemy attacks while maintaining his aggressive playstyle.

Magnum Special

Colt's attack range and Super range are increased by 11%. This enhancement makes him even more dangerous at long range and improves his zone control.

Gadgets

Silver Bullet

Colt's next main attack is replaced by a piercing, long-range shot that destroys walls and deals 2,000 damage. Perfect for breaking cover or finishing enemies.

Speedloader

Instantly reloads 2 ammo and increases reload speed by 30% for 5 seconds. Great for maintaining pressure and ensuring you never run out of ammo in crucial moments.

Strategy & Tips

🎯

Master Your Aim

Colt's effectiveness depends entirely on aim. Practice leading shots and predicting enemy movement to maximize your damage output.

📍

Control Open Areas

Dominate long corridors and open spaces where enemies can't easily dodge your shots. Avoid close-quarters combat whenever possible.

🏃

Stay Mobile

Keep moving to avoid enemy attacks while maintaining your aim. Use Colt's decent movement speed to reposition and find better angles.

🧱

Break Cover

Use your Super to destroy enemy cover and force them into the open. This creates opportunities for easy eliminations.

⚡

Reload Management

Plan your reloads carefully. Never engage enemies when low on ammo, and use cover during reload times.

🎪

Showoff Safely

While Colt loves to show off, pick your battles wisely. Only engage when you have the advantage in range or positioning.

Best Game Modes

Heist

Colt excels at destroying the enemy safe with his long-range attacks and wall-breaking Super.

Bounty

Perfect for picking off enemies from long range and controlling key map areas with his high damage output.

Knockout

Great for eliminating enemies quickly in the small map format, especially with proper positioning.
