Piper

The Elegant Sniper

Sharpshooter Type
Epic Rarity
2,800 Health

Overview

Piper is a sophisticated sharpshooter who combines elegance with deadly precision. As a long-range specialist, she excels at dealing massive damage from a safe distance, making her perfect for skilled players who can master positioning and aim. Her refined appearance and explosive attacks make her both beautiful and dangerous on the battlefield.

Abilities

Attack: Gunbrella

Main Attack

Piper fires a single shot from her sniper rifle that deals more damage the farther it travels. The shot has a very long range and can deal devastating damage at maximum distance.

Min Damage: 518
Max Damage: 1,820
Range: 11.33
Reload: 2.3s

Super: Poppin'

Super Ability

Piper hops into the air and drops grenades in a cross pattern below her. This ability provides both escape utility and area damage, perfect for getting out of sticky situations.

Damage: 1,400
Grenades: 4
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Ambush

When Piper is hidden in a bush, her next attack deals 800 bonus damage. This makes her incredibly dangerous when positioned in bushes near key choke points.

Snappy Sniping

When Piper hits an enemy with her main attack, she instantly reloads 0.4 ammo. This allows for more consistent damage output and better sustained fire.

Gadgets

Auto Aimer

Piper's next shot homes in on the nearest enemy. This gadget ensures a guaranteed hit and is perfect for finishing off low-health enemies.

Homemade Recipe

Piper drops a grenade at her feet that explodes after 1.5 seconds, dealing 1,000 damage. Great for defending against close-range attackers.
