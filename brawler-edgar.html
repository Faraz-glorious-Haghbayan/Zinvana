Edgar

The Parkour Assassin

Assassin Type
Epic Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Edgar is a parkour-loving assassin who can leap over obstacles and surprise enemies with his incredible mobility. This emo teenager brings high-energy combat and aggressive close-range tactics to every fight. His unique ability to charge his Super passively over time makes him a constant threat, able to jump into action at any moment and eliminate unsuspecting enemies.

Abilities

Attack: Fisticuffs

Main Attack

Edgar throws a quick punch that hits at close range. The attack is fast and can be chained quickly, making it perfect for close-quarters combat.

Damage: 756
Range: 2.33
Reload: 0.9s
Attack speed: Very Fast

Super: Vault

Super Ability

Edgar leaps a long distance and lands with a powerful punch, dealing damage to nearby enemies. This ability charges automatically over time.

Damage: 1,000
Leap distance: 5.33 tiles
Auto-charge: 30 seconds

Star Powers

Hard Landing

Edgar's Super destroys walls and obstacles where he lands. This allows him to break through enemy cover and create new paths across the map.

Fisticuffs

Edgar heals 25% of the damage he deals with his attack. This provides excellent sustain during extended fights and makes him harder to eliminate.

Gadgets

Let's Fly

Edgar instantly charges his Super to 100%. Perfect for immediate escapes or surprise attacks when his Super isn't ready yet.

Hardcore

Edgar gains a 25% damage reduction shield for 3 seconds. This helps him survive burst damage and stay in fights longer.
