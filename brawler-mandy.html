Mandy

The Candy Queen

Damage Dealer Type
Mythic Rarity
4,600 Health

Overview

Mandy is a sweet but dangerous candy queen who rules the confectionery battlefield with her bouncing candy projectiles. This sugary sovereign excels at controlling angles and dealing consistent damage through her unique ricochet mechanics. Mandy's candy-based attacks can bounce off walls to hit enemies around corners, making her incredibly versatile in complex map layouts. Her combination of sweet aesthetics and deadly precision makes her a formidable opponent who can turn any candy store into a battlefield of destruction.

Abilities

Attack: Candy Bounce

Main Attack

Mandy throws candy projectiles that bounce off walls and gain damage with each bounce, allowing for creative angle shots.

Base damage: 1,120
Bounce bonus: +20% per bounce
Range: 8.33
Reload: 2.1s

Super: Sugar Rush

Super Ability

Mandy creates a massive candy explosion that deals area damage and leaves behind candy patches that slow enemies.

Explosion damage: 2,000
Slow effect: 30% reduction
Duration: 4.0s
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Sweet Revenge

Mandy's candy projectiles deal 15% more damage for each enemy they've previously hit, rewarding skilled bounce shots.

Candy Coating

Mandy gains a sugar shield that absorbs 25% of incoming damage, providing defensive capabilities for close encounters.

Gadgets

Caramel Trap

Mandy places a sticky caramel trap that slows and damages enemies who step on it, providing area control.

Sugar Boost

Mandy's next three attacks bounce twice as many times and deal additional damage with each bounce.
