Which Political Philosopher Are You?

Karl Marx - The Revolutionary

If you believe in revolutionary change, class struggle, and economic equality, you might align with Marx's socialist philosophy. Marx advocated for collective ownership and the overthrow of capitalist systems to create a classless society.

  • Champions workers' rights and collective action
  • Critiques capitalist exploitation
  • Believes in historical materialism
  • Advocates for revolutionary transformation

Adam Smith - The Free Market Advocate

If you believe in free markets, individual enterprise, and economic freedom, you might align with Smith's classical liberalism. Smith argued that the "invisible hand" of the market creates prosperity for all.

  • Promotes free market capitalism
  • Believes in individual self-interest
  • Advocates minimal government intervention
  • Champions economic innovation

If you believe in pragmatic governance, strategic power, and realistic politics, you might align with Machiavelli's realism. Machiavelli emphasized effective leadership over ideological purity.

If you believe in individual rights, democratic governance, and personal liberty, you might align with Locke's liberalism. Locke argued for natural rights and government by consent of the governed.

  • Promotes democratic institutions
  • Believes in social contracts
  • Champions religious tolerance
