Bonnie

The Cannon Master

Damage Dealer Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,400 Health

Overview

Bonnie is a unique dual-form brawler who can transform between her cannon form and her mobile form, adapting to different combat situations. In her cannon form, she delivers devastating long-range damage with explosive projectiles. When she transforms, she becomes a fast-moving melee fighter with enhanced mobility and close-combat capabilities. This versatility makes Bonnie one of the most adaptable brawlers in the game, capable of both long-range bombardment and aggressive close-quarters combat depending on the tactical situation.

Abilities

Attack: Cannon Form / Mobile Form

Main Attack

Bonnie's attack changes based on her form. In cannon form, she fires explosive projectiles with high damage and range. In mobile form, she shoots rapid-fire projectiles at close range.

Cannon damage: 1,400
Mobile damage: 770
Cannon range: 9.33
Mobile range: 5.33

Super: Transform

Super Ability

Bonnie transforms between her cannon and mobile forms, gaining different stats and abilities depending on the form she switches to.

Cannon health: 4,400
Mobile health: 3,200
Cannon speed: Normal
Mobile speed: Very Fast

Star Powers

Black Powder

Bonnie's cannon form attacks deal 15% more damage for each consecutive hit on the same target, rewarding focused fire.

Wisdom Tooth

When Bonnie transforms, she gains a shield that absorbs 25% of incoming damage for 4 seconds, providing survivability during transitions.

Gadgets

Sugar Rush

Bonnie instantly reloads all her ammo and gains 20% movement speed for 3 seconds, perfect for repositioning or sustained combat.

Crash Test

Bonnie's next attack in mobile form knocks back enemies and deals extra damage, providing crowd control and burst potential.

Strategy & Tips

🎯

Form Selection

Use cannon form for long-range pressure and mobile form for close combat and objective control.

🔥

Focused Fire

With Black Powder, concentrate cannon attacks on single targets to maximize damage output and elimination potential.

🛡️

Transform Shield

Time your transformations strategically to gain Wisdom Tooth's shield during dangerous engagements.

⚡

Sugar Rush Timing

Use the gadget for quick escapes, aggressive pushes, or to maintain pressure with continuous attacks.

💥

Knockback Control

Crash Test can push enemies into walls or away from objectives - use it for positioning advantages.

🔄

Adaptation

Switch forms based on the situation - long-range poke or close-range aggression as needed.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent versatility allows control of both long-range pressure and close-range gem carrying duties.

Heist

Perfect for both defending the safe with cannon form and attacking with mobile form's speed and agility.

Hot Zone

Can control zones from range or contest them directly, adapting to the flow of the battle.
