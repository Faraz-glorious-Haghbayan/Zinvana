El Primo

The Luchador Tank

Tank Type
Rare Rarity
7,000 Health

Overview

El Primo is a powerful luchador tank who dominates the battlefield with his impressive health pool and devastating close-range attacks. As a wrestler-themed brawler, he excels at charging into the thick of battle, soaking up damage, and delivering crushing blows to enemies. His signature wrestling moves and colorful personality make him a favorite among players who enjoy aggressive, in-your-face gameplay.

Abilities

Attack: Fists of Fury

Main Attack

El Primo punches in a short range, dealing high damage to enemies directly in front of him. His attack has a wide cone but very short range, requiring him to get up close and personal.

Damage: 1,680
Range: 3.33
Reload: 1.35s

Super: Flying Elbow Drop

Super Ability

El Primo leaps high into the air and crashes down at a target location, dealing massive damage to all enemies in the area and destroying walls in the process.

Damage: 2,000
Range: 4.67
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

El Fuego

El Primo's Super damages enemies over time, dealing 300 damage per second for 4 seconds. This adds significant burn damage to his already powerful elbow drop.

Meteor Rush

El Primo's movement speed increases by 32% for 4 seconds after using his Super. This helps him close gaps and chase down enemies more effectively.

Gadgets

Suplex Supplement

El Primo grabs the closest enemy and flips them behind him, dealing 1,000 damage. This can be used to reposition enemies or secure kills.

Asteroid Belt

El Primo's next Super destroys walls in a larger area and slows down enemies by 50% for 5 seconds. Great for area control and team fights.

Counters & Matchups

Strong Against

  • Throwers - Can jump on them with Super
  • Short-range brawlers - Outpowers them in close combat
  • Squishy enemies - High burst damage

Weak Against

  • Long-range brawlers - Can kite him effectively
  • Piper - High damage from safe distance
  • Shelly - Her Super can devastate El Primo

Pro Tips

Super Positioning

Use your Super not just for damage, but for positioning. Jump over walls to escape or to reach key objectives.

Wall Breaking

Your Super destroys walls, which can open up new attack routes or deny enemy cover.

Health Management

Don't be afraid to take damage - you have the most health in the game, so use it to your advantage.
