Pam is a support brawler who excels at healing teammates and controlling area with her wide-spread attack. Her healing turret can turn the tide of battles and make her team nearly unstoppable in team fights. Perfect for players who love supporting their team while dealing consistent damage.

Abilities

Attack: Scrapstorm

Pam shoots a spread of nine scrap pieces that deal damage to enemies. The wide spread makes it great for hitting multiple enemies and controlling areas.

Damage: 336 per scrap Range: Medium Reload: 1.5s

Super: Healing Turret

Pam throws a healing turret that heals nearby allies continuously. The turret has high health and can be a game-changer in team fights.

Healing: 1260 per second Duration: Until destroyed Range: Medium
