Belle

The Bounty Hunter

Sharpshooter Type
Chromatic Rarity
3,360 Health

Overview

Belle is a skilled bounty hunter who brings precision marksmanship and target marking to the battlefield. Her unique ability to mark enemies for increased damage makes her an exceptional team player who can turn any ally into a damage dealer. Belle's long-range capabilities combined with her tactical marking system make her invaluable for coordinated team play. Her sharp wit matches her sharpshooting skills in hunting down elusive targets.

Abilities

Attack: Shocker

Main Attack

Belle fires a long-range electric shot that deals damage and bounces to nearby enemies, potentially hitting multiple targets.

Damage: 1,120
Bounce damage: 560
Range: 10
Reload: 1.9s

Super: Spotter

Super Ability

Belle marks the closest enemy, making them take increased damage from all sources and revealing their location to the team.

Damage boost: +25%
Duration: 6s
Mark range: Global
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Positive Feedback

Belle gains 20% faster reload speed for 3 seconds after hitting an enemy with her main attack, allowing for sustained pressure.

Grounded

Belle's Super slows down the marked enemy, making them easier targets for the entire team to focus fire.

Gadgets

Nest Egg

Belle throws a projectile that bounces off walls and explodes after a short delay, dealing area damage.

Reverse Polarity

Belle's next shot pulls enemies toward the impact location instead of bouncing, creating positioning advantages.
