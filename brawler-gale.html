Gale

The Elderly Snow Cone Vendor

Support Type
Chromatic Rarity
5,800 Health

Overview

Gale is an elderly snow cone vendor who uses his weather control powers to support his team and control the battlefield. This supportive brawler excels at area control and team assistance with his snowball attacks and powerful wind blasts. His Super can completely change enemy positioning by blowing them away, while his gadgets provide additional utility and control options. Gale's strategic value lies in his ability to manipulate enemy movement and provide crucial support to his teammates.

Abilities

Attack: Snowball Fight

Main Attack

Gale hurls six snowballs in a cone pattern. The snowballs deal damage and can hit multiple enemies if they're grouped together.

Damage per snowball: 392
Max damage: 2,352
Range: 7.67
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Gale Force

Super Ability

Gale blows a powerful gust of wind that pushes all enemies away from him and destroys obstacles in its path. Great for repositioning enemies.

Damage: 1,400
Knockback: 5 tiles
Range: 8 tiles
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Blustery Blow

Gale's Super charges 50% faster, allowing him to use his powerful wind blast more frequently for better map control.

Freezing Snow

Gale's attack slows down enemies by 20% for 3 seconds, making it harder for them to chase or escape from combat.

Gadgets

Spring Ejector

Gale instantly creates a jump pad that launches brawlers high into the air when stepped on. Perfect for mobility and map control.

Twister

Gale creates a tornado that moves forward slowly, dealing damage to enemies and pushing them along with it for area control.

Strategy & Tips

❄️

Snowball Spread

Position yourself to hit enemies with multiple snowballs. The cone spread can catch grouped enemies for massive damage.

💨

Wind Positioning

Use your Super to push enemies into walls, off objectives, or into your team's range. Positioning is everything.

🌪️

Area Control

Your abilities excel at controlling space. Deny enemy access to key areas and force them into disadvantageous positions.

🏃

Mobility Support

Spring Ejector provides excellent mobility for your team. Place jump pads in strategic locations for map control.

🌨️

Slow Control

With Freezing Snow, your attacks become crowd control tools. Slow enemies to help teammates land their shots.

⚡

Quick Supers

Blustery Blow lets you use your Super more often. Be aggressive in charging it up for frequent area control.

Best Game Modes

Hot Zone

Perfect for pushing enemies out of zones and controlling contested areas. Wind blast can clear zones instantly.

Siege

Excellent for defending against enemy pushes and controlling the bolt spawn area with area denial abilities.

Gem Grab

Great support for controlling the mine area and protecting gem carriers by pushing away aggressive enemies.
