Pearl

The Coral Defender

Support Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Pearl is a defensive support brawler who protects her coral kingdom with bubble-powered abilities. She excels at controlling space and providing utility to her team through her unique bubble mechanics. Her ability to create defensive barriers and enhance team positioning makes her invaluable in team-based modes. Pearl's aquatic theme and protective nature make her a unique addition to any team composition.

Abilities

Attack: Bubble Burst

Main Attack

Pearl shoots a bubble that travels in a straight line, dealing damage to enemies it hits. The bubble has moderate range and can hit multiple enemies in its path.

Damage: 840
Range: 7.5
Reload: 1.5s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Coral Sanctuary

Super Ability

Pearl creates a protective coral barrier that blocks enemy projectiles and provides cover for her team. The barrier can absorb significant damage before breaking.

Barrier health: 3,500
Duration: 8s
Charges: 8 hits

Star Powers

Bubble Shield

Pearl's attacks create temporary bubble shields around teammates, reducing incoming damage by 20% for 3 seconds. Perfect for team protection.

Coral Growth

Pearl's Super creates additional coral spikes that deal 400 damage per second to enemies who touch them. Great for area denial.

Gadgets

Tidal Wave

Pearl creates a wave that pushes all enemies away from her and deals 800 damage. Perfect for escaping dangerous situations.

Bubble Boost

Pearl's next three attacks have 25% increased range and create healing bubbles for teammates. Great for supporting the team.
