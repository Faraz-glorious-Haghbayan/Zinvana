Cordelius

The Fungal Assassin

Assassin Type
Legendary Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Cordelius is a unique assassin who harnesses the power of mushrooms and fungi to eliminate enemies through poison, spores, and biological warfare. This mycological master excels at area denial, damage over time, and controlling the battlefield through his toxic abilities. Cordelius's gameplay revolves around spreading poison effects, maintaining pressure through persistent damage, and using his fungal knowledge to outmaneuver enemies. His connection to the natural world of fungi allows him to create hazardous environments that favor his playstyle. Cordelius combines biological expertise with assassin tactics to become a formidable opponent who can slowly weaken enemies before delivering the final blow.

Abilities

Attack: Spore Shot

Main Attack

Cordelius fires spore projectiles that explode on impact, dealing damage and creating poison clouds that damage enemies over time.

Damage: 720
Range: 6.5
Reload: 1.6s
Poison damage: 200/sec

Super: Mushroom Circle

Super Ability

Cordelius creates a large circle of mushrooms that deal continuous damage to enemies within it and provide healing to Cordelius.

Damage per second: 400
Healing per second: 300
Duration: 8 seconds
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Toxic Spores

Cordelius's poison effects spread to nearby enemies when the original target takes damage, creating chain reactions of poison damage.

Symbiosis

Cordelius gains 15% damage reduction and increased movement speed while standing in his own poison clouds or mushroom circles.

Gadgets

Spore Burst

Cordelius releases a massive cloud of spores that instantly poisons all nearby enemies and reduces their visibility.

Mushroom Dash

Cordelius dashes forward while leaving a trail of mushrooms that explode after 2 seconds, dealing damage to enemies.

Strategy & Tips

☠️

Poison Control

Use poison clouds to control areas and force enemies to take damage or find alternate routes.

🍄

Circle Defense

Place mushroom circles in strategic locations to control key areas while healing yourself.

🔄

Spreading Toxin

With Toxic Spores, target grouped enemies to maximize poison spread and damage potential.

🛡️

Zone Advantage

Use Symbiosis to gain defensive bonuses while fighting in your own poison areas.

💨

Spore Cloud

Use Spore Burst to create confusion and apply poison to multiple enemies simultaneously.

🚀

Explosive Trail

Use Mushroom Dash to engage enemies or escape while leaving dangerous traps behind.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling the center area with poison clouds and protecting the gem carrier.

Hot Zone

Perfect for maintaining zone control with persistent poison effects and mushroom circles.

Heist

Great for defending the safe with area denial and controlling enemy approach routes.
