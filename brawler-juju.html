Juju

The Spirit Healer

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
3,400 Health

Overview

Juju is a mystical support brawler who channels spiritual energy to heal allies and manipulate the battlefield through ancient magic. This ethereal healer excels at providing powerful healing abilities, protective barriers, and spiritual guidance to her teammates. Juju's gameplay revolves around maintaining optimal positioning to support her team while using her mystical powers to turn the tide of battle. Her connection to the spiritual realm allows her to see beyond the physical battlefield and provide supernatural assistance when needed most. Juju combines ancient wisdom with modern tactics to become an invaluable team asset who can keep her allies fighting at peak performance.

Abilities

Attack: Spirit Orbs

Main Attack

Juju fires spiritual orbs that pass through enemies, dealing damage and automatically seeking out injured allies to heal them.

Damage: 520
Range: 6.5
Reload: 1.6s
Healing: 600

Super: Spirit Sanctuary

Super Ability

Juju creates a large spiritual sanctuary that provides continuous healing to all allies within it and makes them immune to debuffs.

Healing per second: 700
Duration: 6 seconds
Charges: 7 hits

Star Powers

Spiritual Link

Juju creates a spiritual connection with nearby allies, sharing 25% of healing she receives and allowing her to see enemies they can see.

Ancestral Wisdom

Juju's Spirit Sanctuary also increases allies' damage by 20% and their projectile speed by 30% while they remain within it.

Gadgets

Spirit Shield

Juju creates protective spirit barriers around all nearby allies that absorb the next 1800 damage they would take.

Ethereal Form

Juju and nearby allies become ethereal for 3 seconds, allowing them to pass through walls and become immune to damage.

Strategy & Tips

🔮

Mystic Positioning

Stay behind cover while maintaining line of sight to provide healing support to your teammates.

✨

Orb Tracking

Your spirit orbs automatically seek injured allies, so focus on hitting enemies while your team gets healed.

🏛️

Sanctuary Placement

Place Spirit Sanctuaries in strategic locations during teamfights to maximize healing and buff coverage.

🔗

Team Connection

With Spiritual Link, stay close to allies to share healing and gain valuable vision information.

🛡️

Protective Barriers

Use Spirit Shield before major teamfights to give your team a significant health advantage.

👻

Ethereal Escape

Use Ethereal Form to help your team escape impossible situations or pass through enemy defenses.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Perfect for supporting the gem carrier and providing healing sanctuary around the mine area.

Siege

Excellent for supporting the team during bolt collection and protecting the IKE turret.

Hot Zone

Great for maintaining team presence in zones with continuous healing and spiritual support.
