Lumi

The Light Bringer

Support Type
Epic Rarity
3,200 Health

Overview

Lumi is a radiant support brawler who brings light and hope to her allies while punishing enemies who dare stand against her team. This epic brawler excels at healing teammates and dealing piercing damage with her light beam attacks. Her dual-purpose abilities make her invaluable in team compositions, as she can keep allies healthy while contributing meaningful damage. Lumi's playstyle rewards players who prioritize teamwork and positioning over individual plays.

Abilities

Attack: Light Beam

Main Attack

Lumi fires a piercing beam of pure light that passes through all enemies in its path. The beam deals damage to enemies and can hit multiple targets lined up together, making positioning crucial for maximum effectiveness.

Damage: 760
Piercing: Yes
Range: 9.33
Reload: 1.5s

Super: Radiant Aura

Super Ability

Lumi creates a healing aura around herself that restores health to all nearby allies over time. The aura follows Lumi as she moves, allowing her to provide mobile support during pushes or retreats.

Healing per second: 480
Duration: 5 seconds
Radius: 4 tiles
Charges: 5 hits

Star Powers

Brilliant Flash

When Lumi's Radiant Aura expires, it releases a blinding flash that deals 400 damage to nearby enemies and briefly stuns them for 0.5 seconds. This adds offensive utility to her healing Super.

Warm Glow

Lumi's light beam attacks also heal allies they pass through for 300 health. This allows her to damage and heal simultaneously, maximizing her value in team fights.

Gadgets

Light Shield

Lumi grants herself and nearby allies a light shield that absorbs 800 damage for 3 seconds. Perfect for protecting your team during crucial moments or objective captures.

Beacon of Hope

Lumi places a stationary light beacon that heals allies for 400 health per second for 4 seconds. Great for creating healing zones at key locations.
