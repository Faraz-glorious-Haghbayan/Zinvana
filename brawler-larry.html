Larry

The Legendary Crawler

Assassin Type
Legendary Rarity
3,920 Health

Overview

Larry is a legendary stealth assassin who excels at surprise attacks and cunning tactics. With his unique crawling abilities, he can navigate the battlefield unseen and strike when enemies least expect it. His mysterious nature and tactical prowess make him one of the most feared brawlers in the arena. Larry's ability to control engagements through stealth and positioning makes him a master of psychological warfare.

Abilities

Attack: Shadow Strike

Main Attack

Larry throws a shadow projectile that deals high damage and marks enemies. Marked enemies take increased damage from Larry's next attack.

Damage: 1,400
Mark duration: 4s
Range: 6.5
Reload: 1.6s

Super: Phantom Crawl

Super Ability

Larry becomes invisible for 6 seconds and gains increased movement speed. His next attack during invisibility deals massive damage and guarantees a critical hit.

Invisibility duration: 6s
Speed boost: +40%
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Silent Steps

Larry's movement doesn't reveal his position in bushes and he gains 20% damage reduction while invisible. Perfect for stealthy approaches.

Predator's Mark

Enemies eliminated by Larry drop a shadow essence that extends his invisibility duration by 2 seconds. Great for chaining eliminations.

Gadgets

Shadow Clone

Larry creates a shadow clone that mimics his movements for 4 seconds, confusing enemies and drawing their fire away from him.

Smoke Bomb

Larry throws a smoke bomb that creates a large area of reduced visibility for 6 seconds, helping both escape and ambush opportunities.
