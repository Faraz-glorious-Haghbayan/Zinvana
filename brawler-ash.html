Ash

The Janitor

Tank Type
Legendary Rarity
6,720 Health

Overview

Ash is a hardworking janitor who transforms into a formidable tank through his unique rage mechanic. His dedication to cleaning up the battlefield translates into powerful combat abilities that grow stronger as he takes damage. Ash's rage system allows him to become increasingly dangerous as battles progress, making him a unique tank who rewards aggressive play. His environmental awareness and protective instincts make him both a guardian and a threat on the battlefield.

Abilities

Attack: Trash Throw

Main Attack

Ash throws a trash projectile that deals damage and creates a debris field that slows enemies who walk through it.

Damage: 980
Slow duration: 3s
Range: 7.33
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Raging Trash

Super Ability

Ash becomes enraged, gaining increased movement speed, attack speed, and damage for a limited time.

Damage boost: +50%
Speed boost: +25%
Duration: 8s
Charges: Rage meter

Star Powers

First Bash

Ash's first attack after using his Super deals significantly more damage, making his initial rage strike devastating.

Mad as Heck

Ash's rage meter charges faster when he takes damage, allowing him to enter rage mode more frequently during battles.

Gadgets

Chill Pill

Ash instantly heals a significant amount of health and reduces his rage meter, providing emergency sustainability.

Rotten Banana

Ash throws a rotten banana that creates a large slowing area on impact, controlling enemy movement.

Strategy & Tips

😡

Rage Management

Build up rage by taking damage, then activate your Super at the perfect moment for maximum impact.

🗑️

Area Control

Use your trash projectiles to create slowing zones and control enemy movement patterns.

⚡

First Strike

With First Bash, make your initial rage attack count by targeting priority enemies for massive damage.

🏃

Speed Advantage

Use your rage speed boost to close gaps, escape danger, or chase down fleeing enemies.

💊

Emergency Healing

Use Chill Pill when critically low on health to survive and continue fighting.

🍌

Zone Control

Use Rotten Banana to create large slowing areas and control key battlefield positions.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling the center area and protecting teammates with his tanky presence and rage abilities.

Brawl Ball

Great for controlling the field and using rage to make aggressive plays around the goal.

Heist

Perfect for defending the safe and using rage to eliminate threats or push aggressively.
