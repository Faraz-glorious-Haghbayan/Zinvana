Frank

The Undead Tank

Tank Type
Epic Rarity
8,400 Health

Overview

Frank is a massive undead tank who wields a enormous hammer and dominates the battlefield with devastating area attacks. This lumbering giant has the highest health in the game and can stun multiple enemies with his powerful strikes. While slow to move and attack, Frank's incredible damage potential and crowd control abilities make him a game-changing presence. His ability to control entire areas and disrupt enemy formations makes him invaluable in team fights.

Abilities

Attack: Hammer Hit

Main Attack

Frank swings his massive hammer in a wide arc, dealing huge damage to all enemies in the area. The attack has a long windup but devastating results.

Damage: 2,240
Range: 5.33
Attack Speed: Slow
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Stunning Blow

Super Ability

Frank winds up and delivers a devastating hammer blow that stuns all enemies in a large area for 1.2 seconds while dealing massive damage.

Damage: 2,800
Stun Duration: 1.2s
Range: 6.67
Charges: 12 hits

Star Powers

Power Grab

Enemies defeated by Frank's Super charge his next Super by 50%. This allows for devastating Super chains in team fights.

Sponge

Frank takes 30% less damage while winding up his attack and Super. This makes him much more survivable during his vulnerable animation periods.

Gadgets

Active Noise Canceling

Frank becomes immune to stuns, slows, and knockbacks for 5 seconds. Perfect for avoiding crowd control and maintaining pressure.

Irresistible Attraction

Frank pulls all nearby enemies toward him. Great for grouping enemies together for devastating Super combos.
