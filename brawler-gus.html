Gus

The Ghost Guide

Support Type
Epic Rarity
3,640 Health

Overview

Gus is a supernatural support brawler who channels the power of spirits to aid his teammates. This friendly ghost guide specializes in providing shields and protection to his allies while dealing consistent damage to enemies. His otherworldly abilities allow him to manipulate the battlefield in unique ways, making him an excellent support choice for team-based strategies. Gus's helpful nature and protective spirit make him a valuable ally in any composition.

Abilities

Attack: Spirit Orbs

Main Attack

Gus fires spectral orbs that pierce through enemies and travel a good distance. The orbs deal consistent damage and can hit multiple targets in a line.

Damage: 980
Range: 8
Reload: 1.6s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Spirit Shield

Super Ability

Gus creates a protective spirit shield around the nearest ally that absorbs damage and provides immunity to knockback effects. The shield lasts until destroyed or expires.

Shield health: 2,500
Duration: 10s
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Spirit Animal

When Gus's shield is destroyed, it releases a spirit that seeks out the nearest enemy and deals 1,000 damage. Great for posthumous damage.

Kooky Pop

Gus's shield also provides a 20% speed boost to the protected ally and regenerates 100 health per second. Perfect for mobility and sustain.

Gadgets

Spectral Sight

Gus reveals all enemies within 8 tiles for 6 seconds and grants all allies 15% damage reduction against revealed enemies.

Soul Swap

Gus instantly teleports to the nearest ally and grants them a temporary shield that absorbs 1,500 damage for 4 seconds.
