Sprout

The Botanical Guardian

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
4,200 Health

Overview

Sprout is nature's answer to battlefield control and team support. This botanical brawler specializes in area denial and strategic positioning through wall creation and environmental manipulation. His unique ability to shape the battlefield makes him invaluable for controlling enemy movement and protecting teammates. Sprout combines offense with utility, making him one of the most tactically diverse brawlers in the game.

Abilities

Attack: Seed Bomb

Main Attack

Sprout lobs a seed bomb that bounces off walls and explodes on contact with enemies or after a certain time, dealing area damage.

Damage: 1,260
Range: 8.67
Reload: 2.3s
Explosion radius: 2.33

Super: Hedge

Super Ability

Sprout creates a wall of hedges that blocks movement and projectiles. The wall can be destroyed by dealing enough damage to it.

Wall health: 3,000
Duration: 10s
Wall sections: 3
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Overgrowth

When Sprout's Super wall is destroyed, it spawns a smaller wall section that blocks movement but can be walked through.

Photosynthesis

Sprout heals 500 health per second when inside bush areas, making him incredibly sustainable in grassy maps.

Gadgets

Garden Mulcher

Sprout's next attack destroys obstacles like bushes and his own walls, clearing paths and creating new angles of attack.

Transplant

Sprout instantly teleports to his wall's location. Perfect for escapes, repositioning, or surprising enemies.

Strategy & Tips

🧱

Wall Placement

Use your Super to block enemy escape routes, protect teammates, or create new angles for attacks.

💣

Bouncing Bombs

Master the trajectory of your seed bombs. Use walls to reach enemies hiding behind cover.

🌿

Bush Control

With Photosynthesis, use bushes as healing stations. Control grassy areas to maintain health advantage.

🚪

Teleport Tactics

Use Transplant for quick escapes or aggressive repositioning. Surprise enemies by teleporting through your wall.

🛡️

Team Protection

Place walls to protect low-health teammates and create safe spaces for regrouping.

⚡

Area Denial

Use your wall to control objective areas and force enemies into unfavorable positions.

Best Game Modes

Heist

Excellent for blocking enemy access to the safe. Walls can protect your team's advance or defend against enemy pushes.

Siege

Great for controlling robot paths and creating defensive positions. Can block enemy robots or protect your own.

Gem Grab

Walls provide excellent map control and can protect the gem carrier while allowing safe escapes.
