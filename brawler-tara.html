Tara

The Mystical Support

Support Type
Mythic Rarity
5,200 Health

Overview

Tara is a mysterious fortune teller who wields ancient magic to control the battlefield. This enigmatic support brawler excels at crowd control and team coordination with her card-throwing attacks and gravity-manipulating Super. Her ability to pull enemies together and set up devastating team combos makes her invaluable in coordinated play. Tara's mystical powers and strategic value make her one of the most versatile support characters in the game.

Abilities

Attack: Triple Tarot

Main Attack

Tara throws three tarot cards in a spread pattern. The cards can hit multiple enemies and deal consistent damage at medium range.

Damage per card: 560
Max damage: 1,680
Range: 7.67
Reload: 1.8s

Super: Gravity

Super Ability

Tara throws a black hole that pulls all enemies within its range toward the center and deals damage. Perfect for setting up team combos and controlling groups.

Damage: 1,400
Pull Duration: 1.5s
Range: 8.67
Charges: 11 hits

Star Powers

Black Portal

Tara's Super creates a shadow portal that spawns a shadow creature with 2,400 health. The creature attacks enemies and provides additional battlefield presence.

Healing Shade

Enemies defeated by Tara or her shadow creature return as a shade that heals Tara and her allies for 800 health per second for 4 seconds.

Gadgets

Psychic Enhancer

Tara's next Super also reveals all enemies for 5 seconds, even through walls and bushes. Perfect for gathering intelligence and setting up plays.

Support from Beyond

Tara instantly spawns a shadow creature at target location with 2,400 health. Great for providing immediate support or distracting enemies.
