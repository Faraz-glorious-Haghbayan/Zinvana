Jessie

The Tech Genius

Fighter Type
Super Rare Rarity
4,800 Health

Overview

Jessie is a brilliant young engineer who combines raw firepower with strategic turret placement. Her shock rifle can chain between multiple enemies, while her deployable turret provides sustained area control. Jessie excels at controlling key areas of the map and supporting her team with continuous damage output. Her mechanical expertise makes her a versatile fighter who can adapt to various combat situations.

Abilities

Attack: Shock Rifle

Main Attack

Jessie fires an energy orb that bounces between enemies and can hit multiple targets. The more enemies grouped together, the more devastating this attack becomes.

Damage: 1,540
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.5s
Projectile Speed: 2000

Super: Scrappy!

Super Ability

Jessie deploys a turret that automatically targets and shoots the closest enemy. The turret provides excellent area control and can be strategically placed to defend key positions.

Turret Health: 3,000
Turret Damage: 800
Range: 8.67
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Energize

Jessie's attack bounces can now charge her turret's Super meter. When the turret is targeted by Jessie's attack, it heals for 800 health instead of taking damage.

Shocky

Jessie's turret shoots electric orbs that can bounce between enemies, just like Jessie's main attack. This significantly increases the turret's damage potential.

Gadgets

Spark Plug

Jessie's turret instantly fires 3 electric spark shots in different directions. Great for clearing out enemies trying to destroy the turret.

Recoil Spring

Jessie's next shot has increased range and will slow down enemies by 50% for 3 seconds. Perfect for controlling enemy movement.

Strategy & Tips

🔧

Strategic Turret Placement

Place your turret in choke points or behind cover to maximize its effectiveness. Think about controlling key areas rather than just damage.

⚡

Chain Lightning Combos

Target enemies that are grouped together to maximize your attack's bounce potential. Each bounce deals full damage.

🛡️

Protect Your Turret

Use walls and positioning to shield your turret from enemy fire. A well-protected turret can control an entire area.

🎯

Support from Range

Jessie excels at medium range combat. Stay back and support your team while your turret provides frontline pressure.

🔄

Turret Synergy

With Energize, you can heal your turret by hitting it with your attacks. Use this to maintain your turret's presence.

🌟

Area Denial

Use your turret to deny enemy access to important areas like gem mine or ball goal. Force enemies to take alternate routes.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling the gem mine area with her turret. Can provide sustained pressure while collecting gems.

Siege

Perfect for both defending and attacking with turret placement. Can control bolt spawns and defend the IKE turret.

Hot Zone

Turret placement in zones provides excellent area control. Can hold multiple zones simultaneously with proper positioning.
