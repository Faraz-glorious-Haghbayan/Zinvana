Frequently Asked Questions

Detailed answers about how the quiz works, what results mean, and how to use them.

Which Wojak are you?+

Finish the quiz to get your closest Wojak archetype match based on motivation, resilience, social energy, and worldview. Your result includes strengths, challenges, and practical tips.

How accurate is this quiz?+

This quiz is designed for insight and entertainment rather than clinical diagnosis. Use the result as a mirror for tendencies, then compare it with your real habits and outcomes.

How does scoring work?+

Each answer gives points to one or more Wojak archetype keys (the labels used on the answer buttons). After 10 questions, the quiz chooses the top-scoring archetype (randomizing ties).

Is any personal data collected or stored?+

No. The quiz runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No answers or results are sent to a server.

I disagree with my result ‚Äî what can I do?+

Retake the quiz focusing on how you actually behave under stress and in your day-to-day routines (not what you aspire to be). Also compare nearby archetypes in the guide.
