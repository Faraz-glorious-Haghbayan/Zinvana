Otis

The Artist

Damage Dealer Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,000 Health

Overview

Otis is a creative artist who uses ink-based attacks to paint the battlefield and silence his opponents. His unique mechanics revolve around ink that can pierce through enemies and apply a silence effect, preventing them from using their abilities. Otis excels at disrupting enemy strategies and providing consistent damage through his artistic flair. His ability to shut down enemy supers and gadgets makes him a valuable asset in team fights and strategic engagements, combining creativity with tactical superiority.

Abilities

Attack: Ink Spray

Main Attack

Otis sprays ink in a narrow spread that pierces through enemies, dealing damage and applying a silence effect that prevents ability usage.

Damage: 560
Silence duration: 1.5s
Range: 7.33
Reload: 1.6s

Super: Masterpiece

Super Ability

Otis creates a large ink splatter that silences all enemies within its area for an extended duration, completely shutting down their abilities.

Damage: 1,400
Silence duration: 3.0s
Radius: 3.33
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Stencil

Otis's attacks slow down enemies by 20% for 2 seconds, making it easier to land follow-up shots and control enemy movement.

Ink Refill

When Otis hits a silenced enemy, he instantly reloads one ammo, allowing for sustained pressure and continuous harassment.

Gadgets

Phat Splatter

Otis's next attack creates a wider ink spread that covers more area, making it easier to hit multiple enemies.

Sticky Note

Otis places a trap that silences and damages the first enemy to walk over it, providing area control and ambush potential.

Strategy & Tips

🎨

Silence Timing

Target enemies when they're about to use their supers or gadgets to maximize the silence effect's impact.

🎯

Piercing Advantage

Line up shots to hit multiple enemies with your piercing ink spray for maximum damage and silence coverage.

🐌

Slow and Steady

Use Stencil to slow enemies, making them easier targets for your team and harder to escape.

🔄

Reload Combo

With Ink Refill, focus on hitting silenced enemies to maintain constant pressure without reloading.

💥

Wide Coverage

Use Phat Splatter in team fights to silence multiple enemies and create chaos in their formation.

🪤

Trap Control

Place Sticky Note traps on objectives, in bushes, or at chokepoints to control enemy movement.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for shutting down enemy supers and controlling the center area with silence effects.

Brawl Ball

Perfect for silencing ball carriers and preventing enemy special abilities during crucial moments.

Knockout

Great for disrupting enemy combos and preventing coordinated team pushes through silence effects.
