Bo

The Eagle Hunter

Sharpshooter Type
Epic Rarity
5,600 Health

Overview

Bo is a wise Native American eagle hunter who brings explosive traps and precise marksmanship to the battlefield. This strategic brawler excels at area control and reconnaissance, using his explosive mines to deny enemy movement and gather intelligence. His unique ability to reveal enemies in bushes and place devastating traps makes him invaluable for controlling key areas and setting up ambushes. Bo's tactical approach rewards patient, strategic gameplay.

Abilities

Attack: Eagle-Eyed

Main Attack

Bo fires three explosive arrows in a spread pattern. Each arrow travels far and explodes on impact, dealing area damage to enemies caught in the blast.

Damage per arrow: 644
Max damage: 1,932
Range: 10
Reload: 2.1s

Super: Catch a Fox

Super Ability

Bo throws three explosive mines that arm after a short delay. The mines explode when enemies get close, dealing massive damage and controlling area access.

Damage per mine: 2,000
Arm delay: 2s
Explosion radius: 1.33
Charges: 10 hits

Star Powers

Circling Eagle

Bo can see enemies hiding in bushes when they are in his attack range. This provides incredible map awareness and prevents ambushes.

Snare a Bear

Bo's mines now stun enemies for 2 seconds instead of just dealing damage. This provides incredible crowd control potential.

Gadgets

Super Totem

Bo places a totem that charges his and nearby allies' Supers over time. Perfect for maintaining pressure and team coordination.

Tripwire

Bo's next Super throws 4 mines in a straight line, creating a minefield barrier. Great for blocking passages and escape routes.

Strategy & Tips

🎯

Mine Placement Strategy

Place mines in high-traffic areas, choke points, and near objectives. Think about enemy movement patterns and escape routes.

🦅

Bush Vision Control

With Circling Eagle, you can see enemies in bushes. Use this to prevent ambushes and gain tactical advantages.

🕸️

Snare Bear Combos

With Snare a Bear, mines stun enemies. Coordinate with teammates to follow up on stunned enemies for easy eliminations.

🏹

Arrow Spread Mastery

Position yourself so multiple arrows hit the same target for maximum damage. Practice your aim and spacing.

⚡

Super Totem Support

Use Super Totem to help your team charge their Supers faster. Place it in safe positions where allies can benefit.

🛡️

Area Denial Expert

Use mines to deny enemy access to key areas. Force enemies to take longer routes or risk explosive damage.

Best Game Modes

Gem Grab

Excellent for controlling the center area with mines and vision. Can protect the gem carrier and deny enemy access.

Snake Prairie

His bush vision makes him incredibly powerful on maps with lots of grass. Can spot enemies others can't see.

Heist

Great for defending the safe with mine placement and long-range poke damage. Can control approach routes.
