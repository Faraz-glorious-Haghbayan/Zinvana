Mortis

The Dashing Assassin

Assassin Type
Mythic Rarity
4,480 Health

Overview

Mortis is a dashing gentleman assassin who brings style and flair to every battle. With his unique dash-based attacks, he can quickly close gaps and eliminate squishy enemies in spectacular fashion. His high skill ceiling and dramatic playstyle make him a favorite among experienced players who appreciate both form and function.

Abilities

Attack: Shovel Swing

Main Attack

Mortis dashes forward and swings his shovel, dealing damage to nearby enemies. This attack allows him to close gaps quickly while dealing significant damage.

Damage: 1,260
Dash distance: 2.67 tiles
Range: 2.67
Reload: 2.4s

Super: Life Blood

Super Ability

Mortis sends forth a swarm of vampire bats that damage enemies and heal Mortis. The bats can hit multiple enemies and provide substantial healing.

Damage: 1,120
Healing: 1,120
Charges: 10 hits

Star Powers

Creepy Harvest

Enemies defeated by Mortis drop a soul that heals him for 1,800 health. This provides excellent sustain and rewards aggressive elimination plays.

Coiled Snake

When Mortis has his ammo full, his next dash range is increased by 75%. This allows for surprise attacks and better positioning opportunities.

Gadgets

Combo Spinner

Mortis spins and damages all enemies around him for 1,300 damage while gaining a 25% speed boost for 3 seconds. Perfect for team fights.

Survival Shovel

Mortis instantly reloads 25% of his ammo (1 ammo bar) and gains a 20% damage reduction shield for 3 seconds. Great for extending combos.

Strategy & Tips

🎯

Target Squishies

Focus on eliminating low-health brawlers quickly. Mortis excels at picking off throwers, supports, and other fragile enemies.

⚡

Chain Your Dashes

Plan your dash sequence carefully. Each successful elimination can reset your positioning for the next target.

🏃

Hit and Run

Dash in for quick damage, then retreat before enemies can retaliate. Use walls and obstacles for cover.

🩸

Use Your Super Wisely

Save your Super for healing when low on health or for securing eliminations on grouped enemies.

🚫

Avoid Tanks

Stay away from high-health brawlers who can survive your burst and counterattack. Pick your battles carefully.

⏰

Patience is Key

Wait for the right moment to strike. Mortis requires good timing and positioning to be effective.

Best Game Modes

Brawl Ball

Excellent mobility for ball control and can quickly eliminate enemy defenders near the goal.

Gem Grab

Perfect for assassinating the enemy gem carrier and escaping quickly with his high mobility.

Bounty

Great for getting eliminations on isolated enemies and using his mobility to escape danger.
