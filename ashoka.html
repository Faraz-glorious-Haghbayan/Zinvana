Died: c. 232 BCE

Notable Edicts: Rock and Pillar Edicts

Transformation and Embrace of Buddhism

Deeply moved by the suffering witnessed during the Kalinga War, Ashoka experienced a profound personal transformation. He adopted Buddhism as the guiding philosophy of his reign, promoting principles of non-violence, tolerance, and moral responsibility. Famous Edicts and Quotes

Ashoka’s edicts are among the earliest written records of governance and ethics. They emphasize compassion, respect for all life, and the importance of truth and non-violence. His words continue to resonate with those seeking a balanced and humane approach to leadership.
