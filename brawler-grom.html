Grom

The Demolition Expert

Damage Dealer Type
Chromatic Rarity
4,600 Health

Overview

Grom is a demolition expert who specializes in area denial and explosive damage through his unique projectile mechanics. His attacks can be charged to adjust their landing distance, making him incredibly versatile in controlling battlefield space. Grom excels at preventing enemy movement, breaking up formations, and dealing consistent area damage over time. His sophisticated understanding of explosives and tactical positioning makes him a strategic powerhouse who can single-handedly control large areas of the battlefield.

Abilities

Attack: X-Ray

Main Attack

Grom throws a bomb that can be charged to control its landing distance. The bomb deals area damage and can hit enemies hiding behind walls.

Damage: 1,260
Min range: 4.67
Max range: 10.67
Reload: 2.0s

Super: Watchtower

Super Ability

Grom deploys a watchtower that continuously shoots at enemies in its range, providing area control and consistent damage over time.

Tower health: 3,000
Tower damage: 560
Duration: 12s
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Foot Patrol

Grom's movement speed increases by 12% for each enemy within his attack range, making him more mobile in combat situations.

X-Ray Vision

Grom can see enemies in bushes and behind walls when they are within his attack range, providing superior battlefield awareness.

Gadgets

Watchtower

Grom's next attack deploys a small watchtower that shoots at enemies for 8 seconds, providing temporary area control.

Radio Check

Grom instantly charges his Super to 100%, allowing for immediate watchtower deployment in critical situations.
