Lily

The Nature Guardian

Controller Type
Mythic Rarity
4,800 Health

Overview

Lily is a powerful controller brawler who harnesses the forces of nature to control the battlefield through plant manipulation and terrain modification. This botanical specialist excels at area denial, crowd control, and creating strategic advantages through her unique plant-based abilities. Lily's gameplay revolves around positioning and timing, using her flora to block enemy movement, create protective barriers, and establish control zones that favor her team. Her connection to nature allows her to transform the battlefield into a garden of tactical opportunities, making her an exceptional choice for players who enjoy strategic gameplay and environmental manipulation.

Abilities

Attack: Thorn Volley

Main Attack

Lily fires a spread of thorns that pierce through enemies and leave behind thorn patches that slow and damage enemies who walk through them.

Damage per thorn: 600
Thorns per shot: 3
Patch damage: 300/sec
Range: 6.67
Reload: 1.9s

Super: Garden Maze

Super Ability

Lily creates a maze of plant walls that block enemy movement and projectiles while providing healing zones for allies.

Wall health: 3,000
Healing per second: 500
Duration: 8 seconds
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Overgrowth

Lily's thorn patches last 3 seconds longer and spread to adjacent tiles when enemies walk through them, creating expanding hazard zones.

Nature's Blessing

Allies standing near Lily's plant walls gain increased damage output and reload speed, making them more effective in combat.

Gadgets

Vine Whip

Lily extends a vine that pulls the nearest enemy toward her, dealing damage and stunning them for 1.5 seconds.

Bloom Burst

Lily creates a large flower that explodes after 2 seconds, dealing significant damage to all enemies within its radius.
