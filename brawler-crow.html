Crow

The Poisonous Assassin

Assassin Type
Legendary Rarity
2,800 Health

Overview

Crow is a cunning and agile assassin who uses poison to slowly wear down his enemies. With his hit-and-run tactics and exceptional mobility, he excels at harassing enemies from a distance and finishing off weakened targets. His legendary status comes from his unique poison mechanic and incredible versatility across all game modes.

Abilities

Attack: Switchblade

Main Attack

Crow throws a trio of poisoned daggers that spread out over distance. Each dagger deals damage and applies poison, reducing enemy damage output and healing.

Damage per dagger: 448
Poison damage: 112 per second
Range: 8.67
Reload: 1.4s

Super: Swoop

Super Ability

Crow leaps over obstacles and lands with a flurry of daggers, dealing damage to all nearby enemies and applying poison. Perfect for escaping danger or finishing off enemies.

Damage: 1,120
Poison damage: 112 per second
Charges: 9 hits

Star Powers

Extra Toxic

Crow deals 140 extra damage with his attack and Super to poisoned enemies. This makes him deadly against enemies who are already weakened by his poison.

Carrion Crow

When Crow has 50% health or below, he gains a shield that reduces all damage by 60%. This gives him incredible survivability in dangerous situations.

Gadgets

Defense Booster

Crow gains a 60% damage reduction shield for 3 seconds. Perfect for surviving burst damage or escaping from dangerous situations.

Slowing Toxin

Crow's next attack slows down enemies by 50% for 5 seconds. Excellent for controlling enemy movement and setting up eliminations.
