Hank

The Bubble Blower

Damage Dealer Type
Epic Rarity
5,040 Health

Overview

Hank is a powerful damage dealer who specializes in area control through his bubble-based attacks. His unique bubble mechanics allow him to control large areas of the battlefield while dealing significant damage to enemies caught in his bubbles. Hank excels at mid-range combat and can effectively zone out enemies with his explosive bubble attacks. His playful appearance hides a formidable fighter capable of turning the tide of battle.

Abilities

Attack: Bubble Blast

Main Attack

Hank shoots a large bubble that travels in an arc and explodes on impact, dealing damage to enemies in a wide area. The bubble has good range and substantial area damage.

Damage: 1,260
Range: 8
Reload: 1.8s
Projectiles: 1

Super: Bubble Storm

Super Ability

Hank creates a massive bubble storm that covers a large area, dealing continuous damage to all enemies within the storm for several seconds.

Damage per tick: 560
Duration: 5s
Charges: 6 hits

Star Powers

Bubble Bounce

Hank's bubbles bounce off walls and obstacles, allowing them to reach enemies around corners. Great for surprising opponents.

Sticky Bubbles

Enemies hit by Hank's bubbles are slowed by 30% for 2 seconds. Perfect for controlling enemy movement and setting up eliminations.

Gadgets

Bubble Shield

Hank creates a protective bubble around himself that absorbs 2,000 damage and reflects 50% of absorbed damage back to attackers.

Rapid Bubbles

Hank's next three attacks reload instantly and have 20% increased damage. Perfect for burst damage situations.
